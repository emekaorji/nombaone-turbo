A **plan** is the thing your customers subscribe to, and its **price** is how much
you charge and how often. You set both up once, from the console, and then put
customers on them.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Plans in the console">
    Sign in and go to **Plans**. Choose **New plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the plan">
    Give it a name your customers will recognise on their receipt: "Pro",
    "Monthly membership", "Premium". The name is the product; the money comes
    next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a price">
    Set the **amount** (for example ₦2,500) and the **interval**: how often it
    bills. Pick a unit — minute, day, week, month or year — and how many of them,
    so "every 3 months" is a month interval with a count of 3. You can add more
    than one price to the same plan, so the same product can be billed monthly
    *and* yearly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a free trial (optional)">
    If you want new customers to try before they pay, set a **trial** of a number
    of days. They won't be charged until the trial ends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save the plan. It's now ready to subscribe customers to, by a
    [payment link](/merchants/share-a-payment-link) or from your customer list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="You can change prices later, safely">
  Adding or retiring a price never disturbs customers already subscribed: they
  keep the price they signed up on until you move them. So it's safe to experiment
  with new prices.
</Callout>

<Callout type="note" title="Amounts are always in naira, shown correctly">
  Enter the naira amount you want to charge (₦2,500). Behind the scenes the engine
  works in kobo for exactness, but the console shows and takes naira. You never
  have to think about it.
</Callout>
