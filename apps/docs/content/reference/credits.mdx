A **credit** is money on a customer's account that is spent before any rail is
charged, oldest grant first. Use it for refunds you want kept on-platform,
goodwill, or prepaid balances. Credits are a sub-resource of the
[customer](/reference/customers), so every operation is scoped to a customer id.

## Operations

| Method | Path | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| `POST` | `/v1/customers/{id}/credit` | Grant a credit (`amountInKobo`, optional `source`, `sourceReference`). |
| `GET` | `/v1/customers/{id}/credit` | List a customer's credit grants and remaining balance. |
| `DELETE` | `/v1/customers/{id}/credit/{grantId}` | Revoke an unspent grant. |

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/customers/{id}/credit \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_test_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "amountInKobo": 100000, "source": "goodwill" }'
```

<Callout type="note" title="Credits draw down oldest-first">
  When an invoice is collected, available credit is applied before the rail is
  charged. The invoice shows the credit line and the reduced amount actually
  charged — see [coupons and credits](/guides/coupons-and-credits) and
  [the ledger](/concepts/the-ledger).
</Callout>
