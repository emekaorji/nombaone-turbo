Yawancin takardun billing suna nuna maka hanya mai sauƙi kawai, su yi shiru kan sauran.
Waɗannan rubuce-rubucen suna yin akasin haka: kowanne yana ɗaukar wani ɓangare na billing
na subscription da yake da wahala da gaske (musamman a Najeriya, inda kuɗi ke shigowa ta
bank transfer, balance kuma ba kasafai yake cika ba), sannan ya bayyana tarkon, dalilin da
ya sa yake da sauƙi a yi kuskure, da yadda Nomba One ke tunkarar sa. Wannan shi ne karatun
da ke bambanta abin wasa da tsarin billing na gaskiya.

## Samun kuɗin daidai

Kurakuran da ke nan su ne masu tsada: an yi wa customer charge sau biyu, proration da ya
karkace da kobo ɗaya, ko sake kunna webhook da ya sake ƙara credit a balance.

## Rail na Najeriya

Zaton cewa card yana kwance a fayil tare da balance cike zato ne na ƙasashen waje. A nan,
ana tura kuɗi ne ta bank transfer, card na buƙatar OTP, kuma charge da ya kasa yawanci na
nufin “ba yanzu ba.”

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Bank transfer ba wani payment method ba ne kawai" href="/concepts/hard-parts/bank-transfer-is-not-just-another-method">
    Dalilin da ya sa ba a iya jan rail da ake turawa, da abin da “karɓar kuɗi” ke nufi lokacin da customer ne ya fara shi.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Card token na ƙarewa" href="/concepts/hard-parts/card-tokens-expire">
    Dalilin da ya sa charge na card mai maimaituwa ke bugun OTP, da tsarin dawowa ta checkout link.

  <Card title="Mandate da izini" href="/concepts/hard-parts/mandates-and-consent">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Lokacin da transfer bai yi daidai da invoice ba" href="/concepts/hard-parts/when-a-transfer-does-not-match-the-invoice">

## Lokaci, cycle, da churn

Billing matsalar tsara lokaci ce, kalanda kuma ba ta jin ƙai: watanni ba su daidaita a
tsawo ba, subscription kuma na ƙarewa ta hanyoyi biyu mabambanta sosai.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Churn na son rai da na tilas" href="/concepts/hard-parts/voluntary-vs-involuntary-churn">

## Multi-tenancy da amana

Idan kana gudanar da billing don ʼyan kasuwa da yawa, ware bayanai da settlement sun daina
zama ƙarin fasali — sun zama duk kayan da kake sayarwa.
