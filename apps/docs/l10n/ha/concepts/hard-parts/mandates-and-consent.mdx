---
title: "Mandate da yarda: siffar bin ƙaʼida ta direct debit"
description: "Direct debit rail ne na jan kuɗi, amma sai bayan yarda ta lokaci guda wadda take da nata zagayen rayuwa."
---

> Daftarin farko. Yana cikin jerin abubuwan da za a yi, amma ba a rubuta shi ba tukuna.

Direct debit rail ne na jan kuɗi, amma sai bayan yarda ta lokaci guda wadda take da nata zagayen rayuwa. An riga an
gina hanyar mandate ta NIBSS, kuma an sanya mata ƙofar live; a rubuta wannan sashe da zarar an tabbatar da shi a production.
