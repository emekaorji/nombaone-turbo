---
title: "Tarkon biyan kuɗi na ƙarshen wata (matsalar 31 ga Janairu)"
description: "Subscription da ya fara a rana 31 bai kamata ya juya ya zama subscription na rana 28 ba a shiru."
---

> Daftarin farko. Yana kan jerin abubuwan da za a yi, amma ba a rubuta shi ba tukuna.

Subscription da ya fara a rana 31 bai kamata ya juya ya zama subscription na rana 28 ta kowane wata ba a shiru.
Lissafin lokaci yana manne da ainihin ranar da aka fara: cycle da aka daure a 31 ga Janairu zai yi caji a 28 ko 29
ga Faburairu, sannan ya koma 31 ga Maris kuma, ba zai taɓa tsayawa a rana 28 da aka rage shi zuwa gare ta ba.
