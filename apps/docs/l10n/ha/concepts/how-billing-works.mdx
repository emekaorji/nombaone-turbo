Subscription shi ne customer da ke kan price, wanda ake caji a kan cycle mai maimaituwa.
Duk abin da Nomba One ke yi yana hidimar madauwari guda ɗaya: **buɗe cycle, samar da
invoice, tattara kuɗinsa, sannan a rubuta gaskiyar a cikin ledger**, sai a sake yin haka
a lokaci na gaba.

## Madauwarin

Kowane cycle yana tafiya haka:

1. **Cycle yana buɗewa.** A kan iyakar zagayen, injin yana lissafa lokacin da adadin da ya
   kamata a biya bisa price ɗin da subscription ke kai a yanzu (yana haɗa duk wani canji
   da aka tsara, proration, ko trial da ya faɗo kan wannan iyakar).
2. **Ana kammala invoice.** Layukan kayayyaki, rangwame, credit, da jimlar duka an kulle
   su. Invoice mai adadin sifili yana daidaituwa ba tare da wani kiran rail ko kaɗan ba.
3. **Ana gwada tattara kuɗi** ta rail ɗin payment method na subscription: ana janyo card
   ko mandate; transfer kuwa yana nuna inda za a biya sannan ya jira. Sakamakon zai zama
   ɗaya daga cikin waɗannan: **an biya**, **past due** (ainihin dalilin faɗuwa da za ka
   rarraba aikinka a kai), ko **pending** (kuɗin na kan hanya: transfer, ko card da ke
   buƙatar customer ya kammala OTP).
4. **Ledger yana rubuta shi.** Ana shigar da kowane motsin kuɗi a matsayin ƙafafu biyu
   masu daidaituwa. Invoice, shigarwarsa, da webhook ɗinsa duk suna raba reference guda
   ɗaya na jamaʼa.
5. **Lokacin yana ci gaba**, sannan a tsara cycle na gaba.

<Callout type="note" title="Idempotent tun daga tushe">
  Gaba ɗayan madauwarin idempotent ne a kan `(subscription, period)`. Ka sake gudanar da
  shi, ka bar shi ya faɗi a tsakiya, ka sake gwadawa. Yana ƙarewa da invoice guda ɗaya
  kacal kuma charge ɗaya a mafi yawa. Wannan shi ne mafi ƙarancin da ake buƙata: injin
  billing da yake caji sau biyu lokaci-lokaci ba shi da wani amfani.
</Callout>

<Callout type="note" title="Inda iyakar take faɗuwa">
  Madauwarin iri ɗaya ne ga kowane zagaye; lissafin da ke nemo iyaka ta gaba shi kaɗai ya
  bambanta. `day`, `week`, `month` da `year` zagayen **kalanda** ne — iyaka tana faɗuwa a
  kan ranar kalanda da ƙarfe 02:00 (Africa/Lagos), wata da shekara kuma suna daidaita
  ƙarshen wata da ranar anchor (anchor na Jan-31 zai yi biya a Feb-28, sannan Mar-31).
  `minute` kuwa zagaye ne na **agogo kai tsaye** — iyaka tana faɗuwa daidai gwargwadon
  lokacin da subscription ɗin ya fara aiki, don haka subscription na minti goma da aka
  fara da ƙarfe 14:37 zai sabunta da 14:47. Duk biyun suna biya ta wannan madauwarin, a
  live kamar yadda suke a sandbox. Duba [ƙirƙirar plan da price](/guides/create-plans-and-prices).
</Callout>

## Injin matakan subscription

Subscription yana wucewa ta ƙananan matakai bayyanannu, kuma matakan da ba sa faranta rai
ana kula da su da muhimmanci, ba wai an ƙara su daga baya ba:

- **`trialing`**: trial na gudana; babu charge tukuna.
- **`active`**: ana billing yadda ya kamata a kowane cycle.
- **`past_due`**: charge ya faɗi; [dunning](/concepts/hard-parts/dunning-for-thin-balances)
  ne ke jagorantar farfaɗowa.
- **`paused`**: an dakatar da billing da gangan.
- **`canceled`**: ya ƙare; babu sauran cycle.

Canje-canjen da dole ka magance su (charge da ya faɗi, customer da ke buƙatar OTP,
farfaɗowa, ko rasa customer) kowanne yana tayar da webhook event da za ka rarraba aikinka
a kai. Ka rubuta handler ɗinka don `invoice.payment_failed`, `invoice.action_required`, da
`invoice.payment_recovered`, ba wai `invoice.paid` kaɗai ba.

webhook ɗin da ke tashi:

## Abin da ya sa wannan ke da wuya a nan

Injinan billing daga wasu ƙasashe suna ɗauka cewa akwai card a ajiye kuma balance cike.
Babu ɗayan waɗannan da yake gaskiya a Najeriya: kuɗi yana zuwa ta transfer, balance ɗin
sun yi ƙaranci, kuma charge da ya faɗi galibi yana nufin *"ba yanzu ba,"* ba *"aʼa"* ba.
Sauran waɗannan shafukan tushen ilimi suna magana ne a kan sassan da suka biyo bayan
wannan gaskiyar: [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger),
[kobo a matsayin cikakken lamba](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo),
[tura da janyo ta rail daban-daban](/concepts/multi-rail-push-and-pull), da
[settlement](/concepts/settlement-and-sub-accounts).
