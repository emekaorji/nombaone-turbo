Kowane adadin kuɗi a API na Nomba One **cikakkiyar lamba ce a kobo**. Babu float, babu
kirtanin lamba mai ɗigo, babu kuma shakku: `₦1 = 100 kobo`, don haka `250000` yana nufin ₦2,500.00.

## Me ya sa kobo, me kuma ya sa cikakkun lambobi

Kuɗi da aka ajiye a matsayin float kuskure ne da ke jiran faruwa: `0.1 + 0.2` ba `0.3` ba ne,
kuskuren kewayewar lamba kuma yana taruwa a tsawon rayuwar subscription. Cikakkun lambobi a
ƙaramin rukunin kuɗi suna sa lissafi ya zama daidai kuma abin dubawa. [Ledger](/concepts/the-ledger)
yana ajiye cikakken kobo, API yana maganar cikakken kobo, kuma su biyun ba za su taɓa saɓa wa juna ba.

## Kowane filin kuɗi yana faɗin rukuninsa

Domin kada a taɓa rikita rukunin, **sunan kowane filin kuɗi yana ƙarewa da `InKobo`**:
`amountInKobo`, `unitAmountInKobo`, `totalInKobo`, `amountDueInKobo`, da sauransu. Idan fili
yana ɗauke da kuɗi, sunansa zai gaya maka rukunin. Ba sai ka yi hasashe ba.

```json
// A ₦5,000/month price
{
  "unitAmountInKobo": 500000,
  "currency": "NGN",
  "interval": "month"
}
```

## Iyakar naira

Ga wuri guda ɗaya da rukunin ke canjawa, mu kuma muna sarrafa shi don kada ka taɓa yin sa da
kanka. Shafin checkout na Nomba kansa yana bayar da adadi a **naira**, yayin da injinmu da
ledger ɗinmu ke amfani da cikakken **kobo**. Da a ce an rikita su biyun, to charge zai yi
kuskure da ninki 100.

Nomba One yana juyawa **a iyakar Nomba kaɗai** (kobo → naira lokacin fita zuwa Nomba, naira →
kobo lokacin dawowa), ba a wani wuri kuma ba. A cikin haɗin naka, da kuma koʼina a wannan
API, cikakken kobo ne kullum.

<Callout type="warning" title="Tarkon ninki 100×">
  Idan ka lissafa adadi a naira ka aika inda ake sa ran kobo, za ka caji ninki 100 fiye da
  yadda ya kamata. Aika cikakken kobo. `₦2,500` shi ne `250000`, ba `2500` ba. Idan ka yi
  shakka, ka ninka adadin naira da 100 sannan ka aika wannan.
</Callout>

## Kewayewar lamba

Proration da rangwamen kaso na iya haifar da ragowar kobo da bai cika ba. Nomba One yana
warware waɗannan a cikin ledger da tsari ƙayyadadde kuma rubutacce, don kafafun su daidaita
zuwa ainihin kobo. Duba [proration matsalar ledger ce](/concepts/hard-parts/proration-is-a-ledger-problem).
