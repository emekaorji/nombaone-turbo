Nomba One na tafiyar da subscription guda ɗaya a kan kowane rail: card, direct debit, bank transfer,
da crypto. Amma rail ɗin nan ba iri ɗaya suke ba, kuma yin kamar iri ɗaya ne shi ne hanya mafi
yawaita ta ɓata billing a nan. Layin da ya raba su shi ne **push vs pull**.

## Rail na pull: mu ne muke fara aikin

Rail na **pull** yana ba engine damar fara motsa kuɗin da kansa a ranar billing:

- **Card**: yin charge a kan token na card da aka ajiye. A Najeriya, charge na card mai maimaituwa
  yakan tarar da matakin OTP/3-D Secure na banki, don haka ba kullum yake wucewa shiru ba. Duba
  [token na card na ƙarewa](/concepts/hard-parts/card-tokens-expire) da tsarin dawo da OTP.
- **Direct debit (mandate)**: jawo kuɗi daga asusun banki da customer ya ba da izini a kai ta
  [mandate](/concepts/hard-parts/mandates-and-consent). Abin dogaro ne, kuma yana aiki shiru da
  zarar ya kunnu.

Ga rail na pull, "collect" na nufin: a gwada debit ɗin, a sami amsa, a yi rikodinsa.

## Rail na push: customer ne yake fara aikin

Ba za a iya jawo rail na **push** ba. Babu wani kiran API da zai kai hannu cikin asusun customer ya
ɗauko kuɗin. **Sai customer ɗin da kansa ya aiko da shi**:

- **Bank transfer**: hanyar da kuɗi ya fi motsawa a Najeriya. A ranar billing, engine na nuna *inda
  za a biya* (asusu na virtual), sannan ya jira kuɗin ya iso.

Ga rail na push, "collect" na nufin: a nuna umarnin biya, sannan a yi settlement lokacin da event
mai shigowa da aka tantance ya tabbatar da turawar kuɗin — ba bisa sanarwa mara tantancewa kaɗai ba.
Shi ya sa [kuɗin da aka tura da bai dace da invoice ba](/concepts/hard-parts/when-a-transfer-does-not-match-the-invoice)
lamari ne na gaske da dole ka tanadar masa, ba wani abu na gefe ba.

<Callout type="note" title="Tsarin gama-gari na rail">
  Tushen injin ɗin bai taɓa sanin sunan wani rail ba. Interface guda ɗaya yake magana da shi,
  "collect don wannan reference", kowane rail kuma yana daidaita shi yadda ya dace da kansa. Wannan
  shi ne ya sa subscription guda ɗaya zai iya gudana a kan card, mandate, ko transfer, alhali ana
  sarrafa bambancin push/pull a ƙarƙashinka.
</Callout>

## Dalilin da ya sa wannan bambanci ke tsara komai

Tun da ba za a iya jawo transfer ba, tsarin "yi charge a ranar billing sannan a sanya alamar an
biya" ba shi da inda zai dace a ciki. An gina Nomba One a kan wannan bambanci maimakon ɓoye shi:
rail na push na yin settlement ta hanyar asynchronous, balance mai sirara na nufin pull da ya faɗi
yana nufin *"ba tukuna ba"* ba *"aʼa"* ba, kuma an daidaita
[dunning](/concepts/hard-parts/dunning-for-thin-balances) domin dawo da kuɗi, ba don sake gwadawa
kawai ba.
