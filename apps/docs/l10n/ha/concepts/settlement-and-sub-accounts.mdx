Lokacin da aka karɓi charge na subscription aka kuma tabbatar da shi, kuɗin ba ya zauna a
tafki guda ɗaya kawai. Yana **rabuwa a lokacin karɓa**, sannan ya settle zuwa inda ya dace.
An gina Nomba One a matsayin multi-tenant tun daga tushe: kuɗin kowace organization nata ne kaɗai.

## Rabuwa a lokacin karɓa

Ana raba kuɗin da aka tabbatar zuwa sassa biyu, waɗanda ake rubutawa a cikin [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger):

- **Kuɗin sabis na dandali**: kuɗin da dandalin ku ke karɓa, wanda injin lissafin kuɗin sabis ke ƙidayawa.
- **Net na organization**: abin da ya rage, wanda mallakin ɗan kasuwa ne.

Net ɗin yana settle zuwa **sub-account na Nomba** na wannan organization. Ana rubuta rabon a
matsayin sassan ledger masu daidaita juna, don haka gross koyaushe yana daidai da kuɗin sabis
tare da net, har zuwa kobo ɗaya tak.

## Sub-accounts

**Sub-account** shi ne asusun organization a kan Nomba inda net ɗin da aka settle ke saukowa.
Multi-tenancy na nufin integration guda ɗaya na iya gudanar da ʼyan kasuwa da yawa, kowanne da
sub-account nasa, customer nasa, da kuɗinsa da aka ware shi kaɗai. Duba
[warewa dukiya ce ta tsarin bayanai](/concepts/hard-parts/isolation-is-a-data-model-property).

## Escrow, refund, da payout

Kuɗin da aka settle ba za a iya cire su nan take ba. **Escrow lock** na settlement na kwanan
nan yana riƙe su na wani ɗan lokaci, domin a iya janye kuɗi ta refund kafin kuɗin ya fita:

- **Refund**: yana juyar da **sashen organization kaɗai** zuwa ga wanda ya biya; ba a mayar da
  kuɗin sabis na dandali, kuma ba a taɓa shi ko kaɗan. Refund yana rubuta sabuwar muʼamala mai
  juyarwa, don haka ledger ya kasance cikakke.
- **Payout**: yana cire balance ɗin organization da ake da shi (net ɗin da aka settle, ban da
  escrow lock da duk wani ƙaramin buffer) zuwa asusun banki.

<Callout type="note" title="Ba a ƙaga, ba a kuma bar kuɗi a tsaka">
  Ana tsare sassan provider na refund da payout: koyaushe ana rubuta gaskiyar ledger, sannan
  canja wurin kuɗi na gaske a Nomba yana aiki ne kawai bayan an tabbatar da shi kai tsaye, tare
  da juyawa ta ramawa idan canja wuri ya gaza, don haka ba a taɓa ƙaga cewa an aika kuɗi ba,
  ba a kuma taɓa barin kuɗi a tsaka.
</Callout>

Domin sanin yadda ake daidaita kuɗi da ʼyan kasuwa a aikace ba tare da spreadsheet ba, duba
[settlement ba tare da spreadsheet ba](/concepts/hard-parts/settlement-without-spreadsheets).
