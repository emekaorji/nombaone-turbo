A ƙarƙashin kowace subscription, invoice, da payout akwai **ledger mai shigarwa biyu**. Shi
ne tushen gaskiya: ba matsayin invoice ba, ba balance da aka ajiye a cache ba, ba webhook ba.
Ledger ɗin. Duk sauran lambobin da API ke mayarwa, daga gare shi ake samo su.

## Debit da credit, ba alama ba

Ana rubuta kowane motsin kuɗi a matsayin **transaction** da aka gina daga **legs** masu
daidaita da juna. Kowace leg tana ɗauke da adadin kobo cikakke mai kyau, tare da **hanya**
(debit ko credit) a kan wani **account**. Hanyar, ba alamar ƙari ko ta ragi ba, ita ce ke
ɗauke da maʼanar. Transaction tana da inganci ne kawai idan debit ɗinta ya yi daidai da
credit ɗinta: littattafan koyaushe suna daidaita, ta yadda aka gina su.

Misali, invoice na ₦5,000 da aka biya yana sanya transaction na `charge` da ke yin debit ga
cash sannan ya yi credit ga kuɗin shigar da ya amince da shi: an lissafa kowace kobo, a
ɓangarorin biyu.

## Me ya sa wannan yake da muhimmanci

- **Babu abin da ke karkacewa a asirce.** Balance da aka ajiye a cache na iya zama ba daidai
  ba; ledger da dole ne ya daidaita ba zai iya ɓoye kobo da ta ɓace ba. Ana karanta balance
  daga ledger, ba a ajiye shi a gefensa ba.
- **Ana iya bincika kowane motsi.** Refund, credit grant, settlement, payout: kowanne
  transaction ne da za ka iya bibiya, tare da legs da ke bayyana ainihin abin da ya motsa da
  inda ya tafi.
- **Juyawa baya wani ɓangare ne na tsarin tun asali.** Refund ba ya soke charge ta hanyar
  sauya shi; yana sanya sabuwar transaction mai daidaita da ke juya motsin baya, don haka
  tarihin ya kasance cikakke kuma na gaskiya.

<Callout type="note" title="Reference guda ɗaya ke haɗa kome">
  Wani resource, rubuce-rubucensa a ledger, da webhook ɗinsa duk suna raba reference guda ɗaya na jamaʼa (`nbo…`).
  Idan kana da reference na invoice, za ka iya samun charge ɗinsa, settlement ɗinsa, da event
  ɗinsa: manufa guda, suna guda, [ko ina](/reference/glossary).
</Callout>

## Idempotency ma a nan yake zaune

Tunda ledger shi ne tushen gaskiya, a kansa ake tilasta idempotency: motsin kuɗi yana ɗaukar
tasirinsa a mataki guda da ba ya rabuwa, don haka buƙatar da aka sake gwadawa ko aka sake
aikawa tana zama rubutu guda ɗaya kacal. Wannan shi ne yadda "[sake gwada webhook ba sake gwada charge ba ne](/concepts/hard-parts/retry-the-webhook-is-not-retry-the-charge)"
ke ci gaba da zama gaskiya: ledger yana rubuta motsin sau ɗaya kawai.
