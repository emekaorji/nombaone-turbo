nombaone API na tantancewa da **maɓallin sirri guda ɗaya ga kowace organization**.
Kana aikawa da shi a matsayin Bearer token a cikin kowane buƙata:

```http
Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…
```

Babu wani client id na daban ko wani matakin sa hannu: maɓallin sirri shi ne ke gane
organization ɗinka, shi ne kuma ke tabbatar da cewa buƙatar taka ce — don haka dole ne ya zauna a kan server kaɗai.

## Tsarin maɓalli

Maɓallin sirri ya ƙunshi farkon da ke nuna mode, sai bit 256 na haruffa bazuwar a bayansa:

| Farko | Mode | Misali |
|---|---|---|
| `nbo_sandbox_` | Sandbox | `nbo_sandbox_a1b2c3d4…` |
| `nbo_live_` | Live | `nbo_live_x9y8z7w6…` |

Ana nuna maka cikakken maɓallin sirri **sau ɗaya kacal**, lokacin ƙirƙira shi, kuma
ba za a taɓa iya dawo da shi ba bayan haka: hash kawai API ke ajiyewa. Idan ka rasa
shi, sai ka sauya maɓallin ka kuma sabunta sabar ɗinka.

<Callout type="danger" title="Ka kula da maɓallin sirri kamar kalmar sirri">
  Maɓallin sirri na iya motsa kuɗi a cikin live mode. Kada ka taɓa saka shi cikin
  git, kada ka sa shi cikin lambar da ke aiki a gefen abokin ciniki ko cikin
  manhajar wayar hannu, kuma kada ka rubuta shi cikin log. Ka ɗauko shi daga
  environment variable ko daga secrets manager.
</Callout>

## Maɓallin shi ke ɗaure mode

Mode ba parameter ne na daban ba: **daga farkon maɓallin ake samo shi**, ana kuma
tabbatar da shi a kowane buƙata. Maɓallin `nbo_sandbox_` ba zai taɓa iya taɓa komai
ba sai bayanan sandbox; maɓallin `nbo_live_` kuma ba zai iya taɓa komai ba sai
bayanan live. Babu yadda buƙata guda za ta ƙetare wannan iyaka, shi ya sa za ka iya
riƙe maɓallan biyu gefe da gefe ba tare da tsoron cewa kiran sandbox zai kai ga live ba.

Duba [environments](/getting-started/environments) domin ganin maʼanar wannan ɗaurin
a aikace.

## Tantance buƙata

Buƙata guda wadda aka tantance, a cikin harshen shirye-shiryenka. Sauya tab ɗin:

<Snippet method="GET" path="/v1/customers" />

Maɓallin da ba a samu ba, wanda tsarinsa ya lalace, ko wanda aka soke, yana mayar da
`401 Unauthorized`. Duba [`API_KEY_INVALID`](/errors#API_KEY_INVALID) domin gyara.
Ga ambulan kuskuren:

```json
{
  "success": false,
  "statusCode": 401,
  "error": {
    "code": "API_KEY_INVALID",
    "message": "Invalid or missing API key."
  },
  "meta": { "requestId": "req_4f9c2a7e1b0d8c3a5e6f10a2" }
}
```

## Sauya maɓalli

Sauyawa na fitar da sabon sirri, ya kuma soke tsohon. Tun da maɓallin yana tantancewa
yana kuma ɗaure mode, maɓallin da aka sauya yana ci gaba da kasancewa a cikin mode
ɗaya: maɓallin sandbox da aka sauya, sandbox ne har yanzu.

<Callout type="tip">
  Ka riƙa sauya maɓallan bisa tsarin lokaci, da kuma duk lokacin da ka yi zargin an
  fallasa su. Ka sabunta duk server ɗin da ke riƙe da tsohon sirri kafin a soke
  tsohon maɓallin, domin gudun samun tazarar da hidima za ta tsaya.
</Callout>
