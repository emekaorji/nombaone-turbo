Yawancin API suna haɗa raʼayoyi biyu marasa alaƙa cikin maɓallin "test/live"
guda ɗaya. nombaone yana raba su, saboda suna amsa tambayoyi daban-daban:

- **Muhallin turawa**: *inda* kod ɗin ke gudana. Alʼamarin kayan aikinmu ne.
- **Mode na asusu**: *waɗanne* bayanai buƙata ke taɓawa. Naka ne, maɓallinka ke zaɓarsa.

| Ginshiƙi | Ƙimomi | Wa ke saitawa | Kana ganinsa? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Muhallin turawa | `development` · `production` | Kayan aikinmu (`INFRA_ENVIRONMENT`) | Aʼa, namu ne |
| Mode na asusu | `sandbox` · `live` | Farkon API key ɗinka | Eh, kai ke zaɓar maɓallin |

## Mode na asusu shi ne wanda kake zaɓa

Mode **yana fitowa ne daga farkon maɓallinka na sirri**, kuma ana tantance shi a
kan kowace buƙata. Ba ka taɓa tura wani parameter na mode ba:

- `nbo_sandbox_…` → mode **sandbox**. Bayanai keɓaɓɓu, babu kuɗi na gaske. Za ka
  iya gwaji cikin aminci.
- `nbo_live_…` → mode **live**. Organization na gaske, motsin kuɗi na gaske.

An raba su biyun gaba ɗaya (bayanai daban, balance daban, webhook endpoint
daban), don haka za ka iya riƙe maɓallan biyu a lokaci guda, kuma buƙata ba za ta
taɓa ƙetarawa daga wannan mode zuwa ɗayan ba.

```bash
# Sandbox: isolated data, no real money
curl https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/examples \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"

# Live: real money movement
curl https://api.nombaone.xyz/v1/examples \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_live_…"
```

<Callout type="note" title="Kowane resource yana ɗauke da mode ɗinsa">
  Duk wani resource da API ke mayarwa yana ɗauke da mode ɗin da yake ciki, don
  haka koyaushe za ka iya tabbatar da gefen da wani rikodi yake.
</Callout>

## Muhallin turawa na kayan aiki ne

Muhallin turawa shi ne *inda* nombaone kanta ke gudana, kuma ba abin da kake
saitawa ba ne:

- **`development`** na gida kaɗai ne: naʼurar da injiniya ke gudanar da sabis ɗin a kanta.
- **`production`** ita ce turawa guda ɗaya da ake yi wa kowane ɗan kasuwa hidima da ita.

Turawar production guda ɗaya tana hidima ga **mode biyun a lokaci guda**. Mode
yana zuwa ne daga maɓallin da ke kan kowace buƙata, ba daga wata turawa ta daban
ba. Babu "sandbox server" da "live server" da za ka nuna. Maɓallin `sandbox` yana
aiki koʼina; maɓallin `live` yana aiki ne kawai a kan turawar production.

<Callout type="warning" title="Live yana buƙatar production">
  Kuɗi na gaske yana motsi ne kawai a kan turawar `production`. Ana ƙin maɓallin
  `live` a ko wane wuri banda haka, don haka ba za ka taɓa yin kuskuren motsa
  kuɗi daga naʼurar dev ta gida ba.
</Callout>

## Aiki tsakanin mode-mode

Haɗin kai mai tsafta yana raba mode biyun sarai:

- **Saiti**: ɗauko maɓallin daga environment variable (`NOMBAONE_API_KEY`) don
  kod guda ɗaya ya yi aiki da sandbox a staging da live a production ba tare da
  canza kod ba.
- **Bayanai**: reference na sandbox da na live sun bambanta; id da aka ƙirƙira a
  sandbox ba zai taɓa aiki a live ba.
- **Webhooks**: yi rijistar endpoint ga kowane mode. Ana kai event na sandbox
  zuwa endpoint ɗinka na sandbox kaɗai.

<Callout type="tip" title="Yi ƙaura da gangan">
  Ka gina ka kuma tantance a sandbox, sannan ka canza maɓallin zuwa `nbo_live_`
  don fara live. Sake gudanar da gwaje-gwajenka na end-to-end bayan canjin: live
  yana da nasa bayanai, nasa balance, kuma yana motsa kuɗi na gaske.
</Callout>
