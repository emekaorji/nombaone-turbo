Abu ɗaya kaɗai kake buƙata don kiran API na nombaone: maɓallin sirri na
organization ɗinka. Wannan shafin zai bi da kai daga sifili har zuwa buƙata ta
farko da aka tantance a cikin sandbox mode.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ƙirƙiri organization ka kuma sami maɓallin sandbox">
    Ka yi rajista, sai a ba organization ɗinka maɓallin sirri na **sandbox** wanda
    yake kama da `nbo_sandbox_…`. Wannan maɓallin na organization ɗinka ne kaɗai,
    kuma an ɗaure shi da sandbox mode: bayanan sandbox kaɗai zai iya taɓawa. Ka
    ajiye shi a gefen server; kada ka taɓa aika shi zuwa browser ko manhajar
    wayar hannu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Aika buƙatarka ta farko">
    Ka tantance kanka da header ɗin `Authorization: Bearer`. Duk `POST` da ke
    motsa kuɗi yana kuma karɓar `Idempotency-Key`, domin sake gwadawa ba zai taɓa
    aiwatar da abu sau biyu ba.

    ```bash
    # Create an example (the worked money-path endpoint), ₦150.00 = 15000 kobo
    curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/examples \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
      -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
      -H "Idempotency-Key: 1c4e7a90-2b3d-4f56-8a9b-0c1d2e3f4a5b" \
      -d '{ "kind": "standard", "amount": 15000 }'
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Karanta amsar">
    Nasara tana mayar da daidaitaccen ambulan: `success`, `statusCode`, abin da
    aka ƙirƙira a cikin `data`, da kuma `meta.requestId` da za ka iya ambata
    lokacin da kake neman taimako. `id` ɗin abin da aka ƙirƙira shi ne
    **madogararsa**: tsayayyen id na jamaʼa wanda kuma ke haɗa rubuce-rubucen
    ledger da webhooks ɗinsa.

    ```json
    {
      "success": true,
      "statusCode": 201,
      "data": {
        "id": "nbo749201835566exa",
        "kind": "standard",
        "status": "pending",
        "amount": 15000,
        "currency": "NGN",
        "mode": "sandbox",
        "createdAt": "2026-06-29T10:14:52.004Z"
      },
      "meta": { "requestId": "req_4f9c2a7e1b0d8c3a5e6f10a2" }
    }
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Adadin kuɗi lamba ce cikakkiya ta kobo">
  `15000` yana nufin ₦150.00. API ba ya taɓa amfani da float ko rubutun decimal
  don kuɗi.
</Callout>
