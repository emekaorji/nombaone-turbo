Ga gaskiyar da yawancin takardun biyan kuɗi ba sa faɗa maka: Nomba sandbox **ba
ya** tura webhook da kansa. Don haka, maimakon mu yi kamar yana yi, muna ba ka
**event na gaskiya mai sa hannu**, iri ɗaya baiti-da-baiti da na production,
wanda kake harbawa duk lokacin da ka ga dama sannan ka gan shi ya sauka a cikin
tunnel naka. Wannan shafin yana tabbatar da bututun daga farko zuwa ƙarshe:
rijista → harbawa → tabbatarwa.

<Callout type="note" title="Na gaskiya kuma mai sa hannu, ba na ƙirƙira ba">
  Event ɗin da za ka harba a ƙasa payload ne na gaskiya mai sa hannu daga sandbox
  instrument, mai siffa ɗaya da tsarin signature ɗaya da na event na live. Babu
  wani abu da aka ƙaga a nan ko aka yi da `setTimeout`. `simulate` shi ne madadin
  na gaskiya ga isar da sandbox ta asali. Yana aiki a sandbox mode kaɗai, kuma
  isarwa aƙalla sau ɗaya ce (ka yi dedupe bisa event id).
</Callout>

## 1 · Yi rijistar endpoint

Ka nuna webhook zuwa URL da kake sarrafawa. A lokacin haɓakawa, ka buɗe sabar da
ke kwamfutarka ta hanyar tunnel (ngrok, Cloudflare Tunnel) sannan ka yi rijistar
URL ɗin jamaʼa:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/webhooks \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "url": "https://your-tunnel.ngrok.app/webhooks/nomba",
    "enabledEvents": ["invoice.paid", "invoice.action_required"]
  }'
```

Amsar ta ƙunshi **signing secret** na endpoint (`whsec_…`), wanda ake nunawa sau
ɗaya kacal. Ka adana shi a gefen sabar. Kana buƙatarsa domin tabbatar da kowace
delivery.

## 2 · Harba event na gaskiya mai sa hannu

Ka nemi sandbox instrument ya aika event mai sa hannu zuwa endpoint da ka yi
rijista. Ka harba na nasara da kuma na gazawa:

Ka duba tunnel naka ko log na sabarka: `POST` na gaskiya zai iso endpoint ɗinka,
ɗauke da jikin event da signature header. Wannan shi ne ainihin zirga-zirgar da
production take aikawa.

## 3 · Tabbatar da signature

Kada ka taɓa amincewa da webhook da ba ka tabbatar da shi ba. Ana sa hannu kan
kowace delivery da `HMAC_SHA256(` `${timestamp}.${rawBody}` `, secret)` bisa
jikin buƙatar da yake **raw**. Ka tabbatar kafin ka yi parse. Ka yi kwatanci a
lokaci mai tsayayye.

<Callout type="warning" title="Tabbatar da baitocin raw, kafin parsing">
  Ka lissafa HMAC bisa ainihin baitocin da ka karɓa, ba bisa abin da aka sake
  tattarawa ba: `JSON.parse` sannan `JSON.stringify` na iya sauya jerin maɓallai
  su canza baitocin, sai signature ya daina daidaita. Ka fara karanta jikin raw.
</Callout>

Ga mai tabbatarwa, yana gudanar da ainihin tsarin a cikin burauzarka da Web
Crypto. Ka manna jiki, timestamp, da secret sannan ka kalli yadda yake lissafa
signature ɗin da sabar take tsammani:

<WebhookVerifier />

Yadda ake aiwatar da shi a sabarka:

<CodeGroup>
  <Tab label="TypeScript">
    ```ts
    import { createHmac, timingSafeEqual } from "node:crypto";

    export function verify(rawBody: string, header: string, secret: string) {
      const [ts, sig] = parseHeader(header); // "t=…,v1=…"
      const expected = createHmac("sha256", secret)
        .update(`${ts}.${rawBody}`)
        .digest("hex");
      const ok =
        sig.length === expected.length &&
        timingSafeEqual(Buffer.from(sig), Buffer.from(expected));
      if (!ok) throw new Error("bad signature");
    }
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab label="Python">
    ```python
    import hmac, hashlib

    def verify(raw_body: bytes, header: str, secret: str) -> None:
        ts, sig = parse_header(header)  # "t=…,v1=…"
        expected = hmac.new(
            secret.encode(), f"{ts}.".encode() + raw_body, hashlib.sha256
        ).hexdigest()
        if not hmac.compare_digest(sig, expected):
            raise ValueError("bad signature")
    ```
  </Tab>
</CodeGroup>

## Sannan ka amsa da sauri, ka kuma yi dedupe

Ka mayar da `2xx` da sauri sannan ka yi aikin ta hanyar asynchronous. Mai
sarrafawa mai jinkiri yana kama da gazawa, kuma za a sake gwada shi. Tunda
isarwa aƙalla sau ɗaya ce, **ka yi dedupe bisa event id**: event guda ɗaya na
iya iso sau biyu, kuma
[sake gwada webhook ba shi ne sake gwada charge ba](/concepts/hard-parts/retry-the-webhook-is-not-retry-the-charge).
