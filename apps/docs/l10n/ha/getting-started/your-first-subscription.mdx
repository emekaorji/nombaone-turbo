A ƙarshen wannan shafi za ka kasance da **subscription na gaske** wanda ke
karɓar kuɗi a sandbox mode, wanda ka ƙirƙira da kiraye-kiraye biyar, cikin
kusan minti goma, ba tare da wani abu na ƙarya ba. Kowane kira da ke ƙasa shi
ne ainihin buƙatar da server ɗinka zai aika a production; API key kaɗai
(`nbo_sandbox_…`) da host (`sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz`) ne ke sauyawa idan ka
koma live.

Hanya mafi sauƙi ita ce albarkatu huɗu da card ɗaya na gwaji wanda sakamakonsa
tabbatacce ne:

1. **plan**, wato abin da customer ke yin rajista a kai,
2. **price** a kan wannan plan: nawa ne, sau nawa,
3. **customer**,
4. **payment method** na gwaji wanda ke yin nasara duk lokacin da aka kira shi,
5. **subscription**, wanda ke haifar da invoice na farko sannan ya karɓi kuɗinsa.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fara samun sandbox key">
  Kowane kira yana tantance kansa da `Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…`.
  Idan ba ka da key tukuna, duba [tantancewa](/getting-started/authentication).
  Mataki ɗaya ne kawai. Ka ajiye key ɗin a gefen server.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ƙirƙiri plan">
    **plan** shi ne kayan da customer ke yin rajista a kai. Yana ɗauke da suna;
    kuɗin yana zaune a kan price ɗinsa (mataki na gaba), don haka plan ɗaya na
    iya riƙe price na wata-wata da na shekara-shekara a lokaci guda.

    <CodeGroup>
      <Tab label="cURL">
        ```bash
        curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/plans \
          -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
          -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
          -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
          -d '{ "name": "Pro" }'
        ```
      </Tab>
      <Tab label="TypeScript">
        ```ts
        const plan = await nbo("/v1/plans", {
          method: "POST",
          idempotencyKey: crypto.randomUUID(),
          body: { name: "Pro" },
        });
        // plan.data.id → "nbo…pln"
        ```
      </Tab>
    </CodeGroup>

    `data.id` da ya dawo shi ne **reference** na plan ɗin. Ka ajiye shi; kira na
    gaba yana buƙatarsa.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ƙara price">
    **price** shi ne ke tsayar da adadin kuɗi da lokacin cajin (interval).
    Adadin kuɗi yana cikin **kobo, lamba cikakkiya**: `250000` yana nufin
    ₦2,500.00. Ka aika shi zuwa plan ɗin da ka ƙirƙira.

    <CodeGroup>
      <Tab label="cURL">
        ```bash
        curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/plans/{planId}/prices \
          -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
          -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
          -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
          -d '{ "unitAmountInKobo": 250000, "interval": "month" }'
        ```
      </Tab>
      <Tab label="TypeScript">
        ```ts
        const price = await nbo(`/v1/plans/${plan.data.id}/prices`, {
          method: "POST",
          idempotencyKey: crypto.randomUUID(),
          body: { unitAmountInKobo: 250_000, interval: "month" },
        });
        // price.data.id → "nbo…prc"
        ```
      </Tab>
    </CodeGroup>

    <Callout type="warning" title="Kuɗi kobo ne cikakke: tarkon 100×">
      Ka aika `250000`, ba `2500` ba. Kowane filin kuɗi yana ƙarewa da `InKobo`
      domin kada a taɓa yin shakka a kan maʼaunin. Aika naira inda ake tsammanin
      kobo yana haifar da caji sau 100×. Duba
      [kuɗi kobo ne cikakke](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo).
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ƙirƙiri customer">
    **customer** shi ne wanda kake cajin kuɗi. Imel da suna kaɗai kake buƙata
    don farawa.

    <CodeGroup>
      <Tab label="cURL">
        ```bash
        curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/customers \
          -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
          -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
          -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
          -d '{ "email": "ada@example.com", "name": "Ada Lovelace" }'
        ```
      </Tab>
      <Tab label="TypeScript">
        ```ts
        const customer = await nbo("/v1/customers", {
          method: "POST",
          idempotencyKey: crypto.randomUUID(),
          body: { email: "ada@example.com", name: "Ada Lovelace" },
        });
        // customer.data.id → "nbo…cus"
        ```
      </Tab>
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Haɗa payment method na gwaji wanda ke yin nasara">
    A sandbox mode kana haɗa payment method **wanda sakamakonsa tabbatacce ne**:
    `behavior` ne ke yanke sakamakon, don haka gwajinka na farko yana yin nasara
    da gangan. Yi amfani da `success` a nan; sauran behavior (decline, buƙatar
    OTP, balance mara isa) suna ba ka damar gwada hanyoyin da ba sa daɗi daga
    baya.

    <CodeGroup>
      <Tab label="cURL">
        ```bash
        curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/sandbox/payment-methods \
          -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
          -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
          -d '{ "customerId": "{customerId}", "behavior": "success" }'
        ```
      </Tab>
      <Tab label="TypeScript">
        ```ts
        const method = await nbo("/v1/sandbox/payment-methods", {
          method: "POST",
          body: { customerId: customer.data.id, behavior: "success" },
        });
        // method.data.id → "nbo…pm"
        ```
      </Tab>
    </CodeGroup>

    <Callout type="note" title="Sandbox mode kaɗai">
      `POST /v1/sandbox/payment-methods` yana nan a sandbox mode kaɗai. A live,
      customer ne ke haɗa card na gaske, mandate, ko umarnin bank transfer. Duba
      [multi-rail: turawa da jawowa](/concepts/multi-rail-push-and-pull).
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fara subscription">
    Ka haɗa komai wuri guda: ka yi wa customer rajista a kan price ɗin, kana
    biya da payment method na gwaji. Wannan yana haifar da **invoice na farko**
    ya kuma karɓi kuɗinsa, kuma tunda behavior ɗin `success` ne, yana dawowa
    **active** tare da invoice da aka biya.

    <CodeGroup>
      <Tab label="cURL">
        ```bash
        curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions \
          -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
          -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
          -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
          -d '{
            "customerId": "{customerId}",
            "priceId": "{priceId}",
            "paymentMethodId": "{paymentMethodId}"
          }'
        ```
      </Tab>
      <Tab label="TypeScript">
        ```ts
        const sub = await nbo("/v1/subscriptions", {
          method: "POST",
          idempotencyKey: crypto.randomUUID(),
          body: {
            customerId: customer.data.id,
            priceId: price.data.id,
            paymentMethodId: method.data.id,
          },
        });
        // sub.data.status → "active"
        ```
      </Tab>
    </CodeGroup>

    `201` tare da daidaitaccen ambulan na amsa yana tabbatar da hakan:

    ```json
    {
      "success": true,
      "statusCode": 201,
      "data": {
        "id": "nbo749201835566sub",
        "status": "active",
        "customerId": "nbo…cus",
        "priceId": "nbo…prc",
        "currentPeriodEnd": "2026-08-03T10:14:52.004Z",
        "mode": "sandbox"
      },
      "meta": { "requestId": "req_4f9c2a7e1b0d8c3a5e6f10a2" }
    }
    ```

    Wannan subscription ne na gaske. `data.id` shi ne reference ɗinsa na
    jamaʼa: id ɗaya yana haɗa invoice ɗinsa, rubuce-rubucen
    [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger) ɗinsa, da webhook ɗinsa.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Abin da ya faru

Subscription ɗin ya buɗe cycle, ya kammala invoice na ₦2,500, ya karɓi kuɗinsa
ta payment method na gwaji, sannan ya sanya motsin kuɗin a ledger: cikakken
[zagayen billing](/concepts/how-billing-works), sau ɗaya. A lokaci na gaba zai
sake gudana da kansa.

## Ci gaba da zurfafawa

Kiraye-kiraye huɗu da ke sama su ne hanyar da komai ke tafiya daidai. Sassan da
ke sa karɓar kuɗi ya zama na gaske a Najeriya suna nan, danna hanyar haɗi ɗaya:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lokacin da charge ya gaza" href="/concepts/hard-parts/dunning-for-thin-balances">
    Balance mara isa yana nufin charge da ya gaza yawanci “bai kai lokaci ba,”
    ba “aʼa” ba. Dunning ne ke da alhakin dawo da kuɗin.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Card na buƙatar OTP" href="/concepts/hard-parts/card-tokens-expire">
    Cajin card mai maimaituwa a Najeriya sau da yawa yakan haɗu da matakin OTP
    na banki. Duba yadda injin ke dawo da lamarin da sabuwar hanyar haɗi ta
    checkout.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Zaɓi rail" href="/concepts/multi-rail-push-and-pull">
    Card, direct debit, da bank transfer suna karɓar kuɗi ta hanyoyi
    daban-daban. Ɗaya daga cikinsu ana turo shi ne, ba a jawo shi ba.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sarrafa webhook" href="/guides/handle-webhooks">
    Duk abin da ke sama yana harba event. Ka karɓe su, ka tantance su, ka kuma
    cire kwafi don ka kiyaye balance ɗaya daidai.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Callout type="tip" title="Trial, proration, da idempotency">
  `POST /v1/subscriptions` yana kuma karɓar `trialDays`, `quantity`, da
  `collectionMethod`; kowane kira da ke motsa kuɗi yana karɓar `Idempotency-Key`
  domin sake gwadawa ba zai taɓa yin caji sau biyu ba. An yi bayanin waɗannan a
  [fara subscription](/guides/start-a-subscription) da
  [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger#idempotency-lives-here-too).
</Callout>
