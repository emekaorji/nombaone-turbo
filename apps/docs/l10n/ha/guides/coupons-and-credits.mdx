Akwai kayan aiki biyu daban-daban da za ka rage abin da customer ke biya, kuma
suna aiki ta hanyoyi mabambanta a kan invoice. **Coupon** doka ce ta rangwame
(kashi ko adadin da aka kayyade) da kake haɗawa da customer ko da subscription.
**Credit** kuwa balance ne da aka ajiye wanda ake cirewa kafin a yi kowane
charge. Duk biyun suna shiga [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger) a matsayin layuka
bayyanannu, don haka invoice kullum yana nuna daidai dalilin da ya sa jimillar ta
kai haka.

## Ƙirƙiri coupon

Coupon yana da `code`, adadin rangwame (kashi **ko** adadin kobo da aka kayyade),
da kuma `duration` da ke sarrafa tsawon lokacin da zai yi aiki:

```bash
# 20% off for the first 3 cycles
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/coupons \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "code": "LAUNCH20", "percentOff": 20, "duration": "repeating", "durationInCycles": 3 }'
```

```bash
# ₦500 off, once
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/coupons \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "code": "SAVE500", "amountOffInKobo": 50000, "duration": "once" }'
```

`duration` na iya zama `once` (invoice na gaba kaɗai), `repeating` (na tsawon
cycle nawa da `durationInCycles` ya faɗa), ko kuma `forever`. Idan kana so, za ka
iya sanya iyaka da `maxRedemptions` da `redeemBy`.

## Yi amfani da coupon

Haɗa shi da customer gaba ɗaya ko kuma da subscription guda:

```bash
# To a subscription
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions/{id}/discount \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "coupon": "LAUNCH20" }'
```

```bash
# To a customer (applies to all their subscriptions)
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/customers/{id}/discount \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "coupon": "LAUNCH20" }'
```

`DELETE` a kan hanya ɗaya yana cire rangwamen.

## Bayar da credit

Credit kuɗi ne da ke cikin account ɗin customer wanda ake cirewa kafin a yi
kowane charge; yana da amfani ga refund da kake son a bar shi a matsayin credit,
ga nuna kirki, ko kuma ga balance da aka riga aka biya:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/customers/{id}/credit \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "amountInKobo": 100000, "source": "goodwill" }'
```

<Callout type="note" title="Ana fara cire credit da ya fi tsufa">
  Lokacin da ake karɓar kuɗin invoice, ana fara amfani da credit ɗin da yake nan
  kafin a yi charge a kan rail, tun daga credit grant ɗin da ya fi tsufa. Invoice
  yana nuna layin credit da adadin da aka karɓa a zahiri bayan rangwame — kowane
  kobo an lissafa shi a cikin ledger.
</Callout>

## Coupon ko credit: wanne za ka yi amfani da shi

- **Coupon**: *doka* ce da ke rage farashi (talla, ko mataki mai rangwame). Ana
  sake lissafa shi a kowane cycle a duk tsawon lokacin da aka ba shi.
- **Credit**: *balance* ne da ake kashewa sau ɗaya, sannan ya ƙare. Yi amfani da
  shi ga refund da kake son ya ci gaba da kasancewa a dandalinka, ko kuma ga
  kuɗin da aka riga aka saka a account.

<Callout type="tip" title="Suna bin juna a tsarin da aka kayyade">
  A kan invoice guda, credit da coupon duk suna aiki: rangwamen yana rage adadin
  da ya kamata a biya, sannan credit ya cire sauran. Tsarin ya tabbata kuma ana
  nuna shi a kan invoice, don haka sakamakon ba ya taɓa zama mai shakku.
</Callout>
