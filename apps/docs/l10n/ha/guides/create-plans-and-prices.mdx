Farashi a nombaone abubuwa biyu ne: **plan** shi ne samfurin da customer ke yin
subscription a kansa, **price** kuwa shi ne nawa da sau nawa. Raba su biyu yana
nufin plan guda (misali, "Pro") na iya ɗaukar price na wata-wata da na
shekara-shekara a lokaci guda, customer kuma zai iya sauyawa tsakaninsu ba tare
da ka sake tsara samfurin ba.

## Ƙirƙirar plan da price ɗinsa a kira guda

Ka tura plan ɗin tare da array na `prices`, za ka kuma sami cikakken shigarwar
catalog a buƙata guda — samfurin da kowane zagayen da yake sayarwa a kansa.
Kiran **atomic** ne: ko dai plan ɗin da kowane price su shiga, ko kuma babu
abin da zai shiga.

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/plans \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -d '{
    "name": "Pro",
    "description": "Everything in the Pro tier",
    "prices": [
      { "unitAmountInKobo": 500000, "interval": "month" },
      { "unitAmountInKobo": 5000000, "interval": "year" }
    ]
  }'
```

`data.id` shi ne ishara ta plan ɗin: a kansa kake tura price.

## Ƙara price

Price yana saita adadin kuɗi (**kobo cikakke**) da `interval` na biyan kuɗi. Ka
tura shi ƙarƙashin plan ɗin:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/plans/{planId}/prices \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -d '{
    "unitAmountInKobo": 250000,
    "interval": "month",
    "intervalCount": 1
  }'
```

`250000` shi ne ₦2,500.00.

Zagayen biya shi ne **maʼaunin lokaci ninki adadi**. `interval` yana ɗaya daga
cikin `minute`, `day`, `week`, `month` ko `year`; `intervalCount` kuma yana ninka
shi. Don haka `interval: "month"` tare da `intervalCount: 3` yana biya duk wata
uku, `interval: "minute"` tare da `intervalCount: 10` kuma yana biya duk minti
goma. Babu `quarterly` ko `ten_minutely` na daban — adadin ya riga ya rufe duk
ninkin maʼaunin da muke da shi.

<Callout type="warning" title="Kuɗi kobo ne cikakke">
  Duk filin kuɗi yana ƙarewa da `InKobo`. Ka tura `250000` don ₦2,500, kada ka
  taɓa tura `2500`. Wannan ita ce [tarkon ninki 100](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo).
</Callout>

<Callout type="note" title="Zagaye da bai kai yini ba yana biya bisa agogo">
  `day`, `week`, `month` da `year` zagayen **kalanda** ne: iyaka tana faɗuwa a kan
  ranar kalanda da ƙarfe 02:00 (Africa/Lagos), wata da shekara kuma suna daidaita
  ƙarshen wata da ranar anchor ɗinka — anchor na Jan-31 zai yi biya a Feb-28,
  sannan Mar-31. `minute` kuwa zagaye ne na **agogo kai tsaye**: yana biya daidai
  gwargwadon lokacin da subscription ɗin ya fara aiki, don haka subscription na
  minti goma da aka fara da ƙarfe 14:37 zai sabunta da 14:47. Ya fi amfani wajen
  kallon zagayen sabuntawa na gaske yana gudana cikin lokacin da kake da shi,
  maimakon [tura agogo gaba](/sandbox-toolkit/clock) da hannu — amma zagaye ne na
  gaske, kuma yana aiki a sandbox da live duka.
</Callout>

## Canja abin da plan ke ci

Ɗaga price na wata-wata gyara ne ga **plan** ɗin — ba farauta ba ne a cikin
layukan price ɗinsa. `PATCH /v1/plans/{id}` yana karɓar array na `prices` iri
ɗaya da kiran ƙirƙira ke karɓa, sannan yana daidaita shi da abin da plan ɗin
ya riga yake ci. Ka tura abin da kake son plan ɗin ya riƙa ci:

```bash
curl -X PATCH https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/plans/{planId} \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -d '{
    "prices": [
      { "unitAmountInKobo": 600000, "interval": "month" },
      { "unitAmountInKobo": 5000000, "interval": "year" }
    ]
  }'
```

Ga kowane zagaye (`interval` + `intervalCount`), engine ɗin yana gano abin da
ya canja:

- **Babu price a kan wannan zagayen tukuna** → za a **ƙirƙiri** price ɗin, kuma
  `price.created` zai tashi.
- **Adadin ɗaya ne** → **babu abin da zai faru**. Ba a rubuta wani layi ba,
  babu event da zai fito. Sake tura catalog ɗinka na yanzu ba ya yin komai,
  wanda shi ne ke sa maɓallin "save" a kan fom ɗin farashi ya zama lafiyayye
  a danna shi sau biyu.
- **Adadin ya bambanta** → za a ƙirƙiri **sabon price**, kuma duk wani price
  mai aiki a kan wannan zagayen zai zama **kashacce**. Za ka sami
  `price.created` da `price.deactivated`.

Zagayen da **ba** ka tura ba za a bar shi gaba ɗaya. A kiran da ke sama, an
sake tura price na shekara-shekara ba tare da canji ba, don haka ya riƙe `id`
ɗinsa; na wata-wata ya tashi daga ₦5,000 zuwa ₦6,000, don haka ya dawo da
sabo:

```json
{
  "success": true,
  "statusCode": 200,
  "data": {
    "domain": "plan",
    "id": "nbo481920374615pln",
    "name": "Pro",
    "status": "active",
    "prices": [
      {
        "domain": "price",
        "id": "nbo903117254480prc",
        "planId": "nbo481920374615pln",
        "unitAmountInKobo": 600000,
        "currency": "NGN",
        "interval": "month",
        "intervalCount": 1,
        "active": true
      },
      {
        "domain": "price",
        "id": "nbo225864019773prc",
        "planId": "nbo481920374615pln",
        "unitAmountInKobo": 5000000,
        "currency": "NGN",
        "interval": "year",
        "intervalCount": 1,
        "active": true
      }
    ]
  },
  "meta": { "requestId": "req_4f9c2a7e1b0d8c3a5e6f10a2" }
}
```

`data.prices` su ne price **masu aiki** na plan ɗin bayan gyaran, kuma koyaushe
suna nan. Barin `prices` gaba ɗaya har yanzu yana gyara `name`, `description`
da `metadata` kawai, kamar yadda yake a da.

### Dalilin da ya sa canji sabon price ne, ba gyara ba

Price **ba ya canjawa**: ba a taɓa sake rubuta `unitAmountInKobo` ɗinsa a
wurinsa ba. Subscription yana riƙe da `priceId` guda, kuma wannan layin da aka
riƙe shi ne kaɗai dalilin da ya sa kuɗin wanda ya riga ya yi rijista ba zai iya
motsawa a ƙarƙashinsa ba. Don haka canji yana ƙirƙirar **sabon** layi ya kuma
ajiye tsohon — wanda ya tsaya daidai yadda yake, yana faɗin abin da masu
biyanka na yanzu suka yarda su biya. Grandfathering halayya ce ta tsarin data,
ba wani abu da wani ya tuna ya gina ba. Sababbi suna kaiwa ga sabon price;
waɗanda tuni suke biya suna riƙe da nasu har sai ka matsar da su da gangan,
wanda shi ne [canjin plan](/guides/proration-and-plan-changes).

## Kalli subscription yana biya

Subscription na wata-wata yana ɗaukar wata guda kafin ya tabbatar yana
sabuntawa. `interval: "minute"` tare da `intervalCount: 10` yana tabbatar da
shi cikin lokacin abincin rana:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/plans/{planId}/prices \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "unitAmountInKobo": 50000, "interval": "minute", "intervalCount": 10 }'
```

Ka sa customer a kan wannan price ɗin, bayan minti goma sabuntawar za ta faru
da kanta, ba tare da wani abin ƙarya ba: `invoice.created`,
`invoice.finalized`, cajin, sannan `invoice.paid`, rubuce-rubucen ledger, kuma
lokacin subscription ɗin zai mirgina gaba. Engine **ɗaya** da `month` ke bi ne
yake bi — zagayen ne kaɗai ya bambanta. Zagaye ne na gaske kuma: yana aiki a
live, ba sandbox kaɗai ba. Lura: sabuntawa tana sauka ne lokacin da billing
sweep ya gudana, don haka zagayen minti yana biya a kan lokaci ne kawai idan
sweep ɗin yana bugawa kowane minti (`BILLING_SWEEP_CRON=* * * * *`, wanda
yanzu shi ne tsoho).

## Ƙara trial

Ka saita `trialPeriodDays` a kan price. Subscription da aka fara da shi yana shiga
`trialing`: babu charge sai trial ɗin ya ƙare, sannan a karɓi invoice na farko.

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/plans/{planId}/prices \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "unitAmountInKobo": 250000, "interval": "month", "trialPeriodDays": 14 }'
```

## Price da yawa a kan plan guda

Ka ƙirƙiri price gwargwadon yadda kake sayar da plan ɗin: na wata-wata da na
shekara-shekara, matakan NGN, farashin tallatawa. Subscription yana nuni ga
**price**, ba plan ba, don haka mayar da customer daga wata-wata zuwa
shekara-shekara [canjin plan](/guides/proration-and-plan-changes) ne zuwa wani
price daban a kan plan ɗin guda, mu kuma za mu kula da proration.

## Adanawa

Ana adana plan (`POST /v1/plans/{id}/archive`) ana kuma kashe price
(`POST /v1/prices/{id}/deactivate`) domin ka daina karɓar sabbin subscription a
kansu ba tare da taɓa waɗanda tuni suke biya ba. Subscription ɗin da suke nan za
su riƙe price ɗinsu; adanawa yana hana sabon amfani ne kawai.
