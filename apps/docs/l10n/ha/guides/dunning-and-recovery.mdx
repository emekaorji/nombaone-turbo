Lokacin da charge ya faɗi, yawancin tsarin biyan kuɗi na ɗauka cewa amsar "aʼa" ce.
A Najeriya, gaskiyar magana ita ce **"ba yanzu ba"**: balance ɗin ya yi ƙaranci amma
za a ƙara masa kuɗi, ko kuma card ɗin na buƙatar OTP na sau ɗaya. Dunning shi ne
madauwarin farfaɗowa da injin ke amfani da shi domin wannan daidai, kuma an daidaita
shi don dawo da kuɗi, ba wai kawai sake gwadawa a makance ba. Duba
[dunning don balance mai ƙaranci](/concepts/hard-parts/dunning-for-thin-balances) don
dalilin; wannan jagorar tana nuna yadda za ka gudanar da shi.

## Abin da ke faruwa idan charge ya faɗi

Idan tattara kuɗin cycle ya faɗi, subscription yana matsawa zuwa `past_due` kuma
dunning ya karɓi ragamar aikin ta atomatik:

1. `invoice.payment_failed` yana tashi tare da **ainihin dalilin faɗuwa** da za ka
   rarraba aikinka a kai (rashin isasshen kuɗi, card ya ƙare, ana buƙatar OTP, ba
   wai "declined" na gama-gari ba).
2. Dunning yana tsara sake gwadawa a kan lokacinsa, ba sai ka yi komai ba.
3. Kowane sake gwadawa ko dai ya dawo da kuɗi (→ `invoice.payment_recovered`,
   subscription ya zama `active`) ko kuma ya gama jadawalin (→ subscription ya ci
   gaba da kasancewa `past_due` ko kuma a soke shi, bisa ga manufarka).

<Callout type="warning" title="Kada ka yanke damar shiga a faɗuwa ta farko">
  `past_due` ba `canceled` ba ne. A kan balance mai ƙaranci, sake gwadawa yakan yi
  nasara cikin ʼyan saʼoʼi. Yanke damar shiga a kan `invoice.payment_failed` yana
  korar customer waɗanda da sun biya. Ka jira jadawalin dunning ya ƙare, ko kuma ka
  jira `subscription.canceled`.
</Callout>

## Reshen card-OTP

Charge na **card** mai maimaituwa a Najeriya yakan haifar da matakin OTP/3-D Secure
na banki wanda ba za a iya kammala shi ba tare da mutum ba. Idan haka ta faru,
dunning ba ya ci gaba da sake gwadawa a shiru har abada. Yana fitar da
`invoice.action_required` tare da **sabuwar hanyar checkout**. Ka aika da customer
zuwa can; idan ya kammala OTP a wurin, invoice ɗin zai sasanta kuma subscription zai
farfaɗo. Wannan ita ce amsar da samfurinmu ya bayar ga rail da ba za a iya janyo kuɗi
a kansa a shiru ba. Duba [token na card suna ƙarewa](/concepts/hard-parts/card-tokens-expire).

## Duba yanayin dunning

Karanta inda farfaɗowa ta kai ga wani subscription:

```bash
# Current dunning status
curl https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions/{id}/dunning \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"

# The full attempt history
curl https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions/{id}/dunning/attempts \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"
```

## Gwada shi a test

Ka haɗa payment method na test wanda ke faɗuwa, sannan ka kalli yadda madauwarin ke
gudana:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/sandbox/payment-methods \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "customerId": "{customerId}", "behavior": "decline_insufficient_funds" }'
```

Yi amfani da `requires_otp` don gwada reshen checkout-link, kuma
[ka tabbatar da mu a cikin devtools ɗinka](/getting-started/verify-in-your-devtools)
don fitar da event `invoice.action_required` zuwa endpoint ɗinka.

## Event ɗin da za ka sarrafa

- **`invoice.payment_failed`**: an shiga dunning. Ka yi rikodinsa; kada ka kori customer tukuna.
- **`invoice.action_required`**: ka aika wa customer hanyar checkout da ke cikin payload.
- **`invoice.payment_recovered`**: ka mayar da damar shiga kamar yadda take.
- **`subscription.canceled`**: dunning ya ƙare (idan manufarka ta soke). Yanzu ka yanke damar shiga.
