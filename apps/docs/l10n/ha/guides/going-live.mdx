An yi niyyar sa komawa live ya zama ƙarami: ka canza key ɗinka na `nbo_sandbox_` zuwa `nbo_live_`, ka
kuma canza host ɗinka daga `sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz` zuwa `api.nombaone.xyz`. Amma mode
biyun ba sa raba komai (kowanne da data, balance, da webhook endpoint nasa), don haka
ka bi wannan jerin binciken kafin charge na gaske na farko.

## Canjin

Farkon key ɗin shi ne ke ƙayyade komai. Babu wani parameter na mode: key na `nbo_live_`
yana kaiwa live kaɗai, key na `nbo_sandbox_` kuwa sandbox kaɗai. Duba
[environments](/getting-started/environments).

```bash
# Sandbox
https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1   +   nbo_sandbox_…

# Live
https://api.nombaone.xyz/v1           +   nbo_live_…
```

Ka ɗauko key ɗin daga environment variable domin kod ɗin nan guda ya yi aiki a kan biyun:

```ts
const base = process.env.NOMBAONE_BASE_URL;   // sandbox vs prod
const key  = process.env.NOMBAONE_API_KEY; // nbo_sandbox_ vs nbo_live_
```

## Jerin bincike kafin ƙaddamarwa

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sake ƙirƙirar plan da price ɗinka a live">
    Data na sandbox da na live sun rabu sarai: plan id da aka ƙirƙira a sandbox babu shi
    a live. Sake ƙirƙirar plan, price, da coupon ɗinka da key na live
    (ko ka gudanar da seed script ɗinka a kan base na live).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Yi rijistar webhook endpoint na live">
    Webhook na kowane mode daban ne. Yi rijistar endpoint ɗinka na production da key na
    live, ka kuma ajiye sabon secret ɗin `whsec_…`. Ya bambanta da secret ɗinka na
    sandbox. Ka tabbatar da signature ta amfani da secret na live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tabbatar da bincikar signature da secret na live">
    Ka aika da event na gaske ka tabbatar handler ɗinka yana bincika shi: HMAC a kan raw
    body, kwatance mai ɗaukar lokaci ɗaya kullum. Handler da ya yi nasara a sandbox da
    secret na sandbox zai ƙi event na live har sai ka mai da shi ga secret na live. Duba
    [sarrafa webhook](/guides/handle-webhooks).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tabbatar da maʼaunin kuɗi daga farko har ƙarshe">
    Kowane adadi yana cikin **kobo na cikakken lamba**. Kafin charge na farko, ka tabbatar
    price da ka sani yana nuna adadin naira daidai a UI ɗinka (`250000` → ₦2,500.00). Wannan
    shi ne binciken da ke hana caji sau 100 fiye da kima. Duba
    [kuɗi kobo ne na cikakken lamba](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Saka Idempotency-Key a kowace kira da ke motsa kuɗi">
    Sake gwada aikawa saboda matsalar sadarwa ba makawa ne a production. Ka tabbatar kowane
    `POST` da ke motsa kuɗi yana aika `Idempotency-Key` na musamman, domin sake gwadawa kada ya caje sau biyu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sarrafa hanyoyin da abubuwa ba sa tafiya daidai">
    Ka tabbatar handler ɗinka na webhook yana da rassa domin `invoice.payment_failed`,
    `invoice.action_required`, da `invoice.payment_recovered`, ba `invoice.paid`
    kaɗai ba. A Najeriya, hanyoyin da abubuwa ba sa tafiya daidai su ne suka fi yawa.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Live yana motsa kuɗin gaske tun daga charge na farko">
  Babu gwajin banza a live. Subscription ɗinka na farko a live zai tara kuɗin gaske
  ta rail na gaske. Ka gudanar da maʼamala guda daga farko zuwa ƙarshe da ƙaramin adadi da
  payment method na gaske da ke hannunka kafin ka shigar da customer.
</Callout>

## Bayan ƙaddamarwa

- **Yi reconciliation.** [Ledger](/concepts/the-ledger) shi ne tushen gaskiya; idan webhook
  ya ɓace, sake ɗebo resource ɗin maimakon sake gina yanayin da hannu.
- **Kula da dunning.** Balance na gaske kaɗan ne: ka yi tsammanin `past_due` ka bar
  [dunning](/guides/dunning-and-recovery) ya kwato kuɗin kafin ka yanke damar shiga.
- **Payout.** Kuɗin da aka settle yana saukowa a sub-account na organization ƙarƙashin kullen
  escrow; ka fitar da shi da [payout](/guides/refunds-payouts-settlement).
