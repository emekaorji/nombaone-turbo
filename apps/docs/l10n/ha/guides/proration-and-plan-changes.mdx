Da wuya customer ya tsaya kan price guda har abada. Suna haɓakawa, suna ragewa,
suna ƙara kujeru, ko su canza daga wata-wata zuwa shekara-shekara — galibi **a
tsakiyar cycle**, bayan sun riga sun biya kuɗin wani ɓangare na lokacin. Canjin
plan yana matsar da subscription zuwa sabon price kuma ya daidaita bambancin,
kuma tun da
[proration matsalar ledger ce](/concepts/hard-parts/proration-is-a-ledger-problem),
ana lissafa shi da ƙafafun kobo masu daidaituwa, ba tsammani na floating-point ba.

## Canza price

Ka nuna wa subscription wani price daban. Kira guda ɗaya yana kula da canjin da
proration tare:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions/{id}/change \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -d '{ "priceId": "{newPriceId}", "prorationBehavior": "create_prorations" }'
```

Injin yana mayar da kuɗin ɓangaren tsohon price da ba a yi amfani da shi ba, sannan
ya caji ɓangaren sabon price da aka yi amfani da shi, har zuwa kobo ɗaya tak. A kan
**haɓaka**, customer yana biyan bambancin nan take; a kan **ragewa**, credit ɗin
yana shiga invoice na gaba.

## Duba tukun kafin ka tabbatar

Nuna wa customer ainihin kuɗin da canji zai ci kafin ka aiwatar da shi:

```bash
curl https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions/{id}/upcoming-invoice \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"
```

Invoice mai zuwa yana nuna layukan proration, don haka babu abin mamaki a kan
biyan kuɗi na gaba.

## Canza zagayen biya

Matsawa daga wata-wata → shekara-shekara canji ne zuwa price mai zagaye daban a kan
plan guda. Zagaye **maʼauni ne ninki adadi**, don haka wata-wata → kowane wata uku
(`month` × 3) da wata-wata → kowane minti goma (`minute` × 10) su ma canjin zagaye ne —
canjin adadin yana da nauyi kamar canjin maʼaunin:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions/{id}/change \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "priceId": "{yearlyPriceId}", "intervalSwitch": true }'
```

Injin yana gane canjin zagaye da kansa, daga price ɗin biyu — ba sai ka sanar da shi ba.
Maimakon ya yi prorate na shekara a kan wata, yana mayar da saura na zagayen da ya wuce
a matsayin credit, ya caji cikakken sabon zagaye, sannan ya sake saita anchor ɗin cycle.
`intervalSwitch: true` yana neman wannan magani iri ɗaya lokacin da zagayen **bai**
canza ba.

## Sarrafa halin proration

- **`create_prorations`**: lissafa bambancin sannan a yi amfani da shi (shi ne tsoho
  don haɓakawa).
- **`none`**: canza price ba tare da proration ba; sabon adadin zai fara aiki daga
  cycle na gaba.

<Callout type="note" title="Canjin adadi ma yana prorate">
  Ƙara kujeru ko cire su a tsakiyar cycle hanya ɗaya ce: ka tura `quantity` zuwa
  `change`, sannan bambancin kowace kujera yana prorate daidai kamar canjin price.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" title="Tsara canji don sabuntawa na gaba">
  Domin gujewa proration na tsakiyar cycle gaba ɗaya, ka tsara canjin ya fara aiki a
  iyakar lokacin da ke gaba ta hanyar `POST /v1/subscriptions/{id}/schedule`.
  Customer zai kammala lokacin da ya biya, sannan ya sabunta a kan sabon price.
</Callout>
