Idan aka karɓi charge aka kuma tabbatar da shi, kuɗin yana **rabuwa a lokacin
karɓa**: kuɗin platform naka da net na organization, kowanne a matsayin ƙafafun
[ledger](/concepts/the-ledger) masu daidaito. Wannan jagora yana nuna yadda kuɗi
ke sake fita waje: refund, escrow lock da ke tsare shi, da payout. An bayyana
tsarin a [settlement & sub-accounts](/concepts/settlement-and-sub-accounts); ga
yadda ake sarrafa shi.

## Yi refund kan settlement

Refund yana mayar da charge da aka settle zuwa ga wanda ya biya. Ta tsohuwa yana
mayar da cikakken net; ka tura `amountInKobo` idan kana son mayar da ɓangare:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/settlements/{id}/refund \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -d '{ "amountInKobo": 250000 }'
```

<Callout type="note" title="Ba a mayar da kuɗin platform">
  Refund yana juya **ƙafar organization kaɗai**: yana rage settlement balance na
  organization sannan ya mayar da kuɗin ga mai biya. Ba a mayar da kuɗin
  platform, ba a kuma taɓa shi ko kaɗan. Wannan shi ke sa ledger ya ci gaba da
  zama na gaskiya: refund yana shigar da sabon transaction mai juyawa, ba ya
  sauya charge na asali.
</Callout>

Refund na ɓangare yana taruwa: ka mayar da ₦1,000 daga charge na ₦2,500, sannan
daga baya ka mayar da ₦1,500, settlement ɗin kuwa sai ya juya zuwa `refunded`
gaba ɗaya. Ba za ka taɓa iya mayar da fiye da net ba. API zai ƙi hakan, kuma
`Idempotency-Key` yana sa sake gwada refund ya zama mai aminci.

## Karanta escrow lock

Kuɗin da aka settle ba a iya cire su nan take. **Escrow lock** na settlement na
kwanan nan yana riƙe su na wani ɗan lokaci domin a iya janye refund kafin kuɗin
ya fita. Duba abin da aka kulle da abin da ake da shi a hannu:

```bash
curl https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/settlements/escrow \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"
```

Amsar tana nuna adadin da aka kulle da lokacin da za a sake shi. Balance da ake
da shi domin payout shi ne net da aka settle bayan an cire escrow lock (da duk
wani mafi ƙarancin ajiya).

## Biya zuwa asusun banki

Cire balance da organization ke da shi zuwa banki:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/settlements/payout \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -d '{ "amountInKobo": 500000, "bankCode": "058", "accountNumber": "0123456789" }'
```

Masu tsaro biyu suna kare kuɗin:

- **`ESCROW_LOCKED`**: ka yi ƙoƙarin cire kuɗin da har yanzu yake cikin lokacin
  escrow. Ka jira a sake shi, ko ka cire ɓangaren da ake da shi kawai.
- **`PAYOUT_EXCEEDS_AVAILABLE`**: adadin ya fi balance da ake da shi.

<Callout type="warning" title="Gaskiyar ledger da farko, turawa daga baya">
  Payout yana shigar da debit a ledger kafin a gwada tura kuɗi zuwa banki, kuma
  idan turawar ta gaza sai ya shigar da juyawa ta ramuwa. Ba a taɓa yin ƙarya
  cewa an tura kuɗi ba, kuma ba a bar kuɗi a rataye: ledger da banki suna ci
  gaba da daidaita.
</Callout>
