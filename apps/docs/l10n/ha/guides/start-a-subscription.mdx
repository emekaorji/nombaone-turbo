Subscription yana sanya **customer** a kan **price**, kuma yana caji shi kowane
cycle. Subscription resource ba ya damuwa da ko wane rail ne: `POST /v1/subscriptions`
guda ɗaya ne ke fara subscription na card, na direct debit, ko na bank transfer.
Abin da ya bambanta shi ne **payment method** da ka haɗa, domin rails na karɓar
kuɗi ta hanyoyi daban-daban: card da mandate ana *jansu* ne, bank transfer kuwa
ana *turo* shi. Duba [multi-rail: push da pull](/concepts/multi-rail-push-and-pull)
don ka ga dalilin da ya sa wannan bambanci yake da muhimmanci.

## Saita payment method

Pull rails suna buƙatar kayan biya da aka riga aka ba da izininsa, wanda aka ajiye.
Fara setup: customer ɗin zai ba da izini (kuma, ga card, wataƙila ya kammala
matakin OTP na banki) a mahaɗin da aka mayar:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/payment-methods/setup \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "customerRef": "{customerId}",
    "amountInKobo": 250000,
    "callbackUrl": "https://yourapp.com/billing/return"
  }'
```

Ana tura customer ɗin ya kammala ba da izini, sannan a mayar da shi zuwa
`callbackUrl` ɗinka. Payment method ɗin da ya fito daga nan shi ne abin da za ka yi
caji a kansa kowane cycle.

Ga subscription na **bank transfer**, sai ka ƙirƙiri virtual account maimakon haka:
customer ɗin zai turo kuɗi zuwa gare shi a kowace ranar biyan kuɗi:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/payment-methods/virtual-account \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "customerRef": "{customerId}", "expectedAmount": 250000 }'
```

<Callout type="note" title="Yi gwaji mai sakamako tabbatacce a sandbox">
  A cikin sandbox, ka tsallake matakin shafin da muke bayarwa:
  `POST /v1/sandbox/payment-methods` tare da `behavior`
  (`success`, `requires_otp`, `decline_insufficient_funds`) yana ba ka method wanda
  aka riga aka san sakamakonsa, don haka za ka iya gwada hanya mai kyau da wadda ba
  ta yi kyau ba kafin kuɗi na gaske ya shiga ciki.
</Callout>

## Fara subscription ɗin

Yi wa customer ɗin rijista a kan price ɗin da wannan method:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/subscriptions \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "Idempotency-Key: $(uuidgen)" \
  -d '{
    "customerId": "{customerId}",
    "priceId": "{priceId}",
    "paymentMethodId": "{paymentMethodId}",
    "collectionMethod": "charge_automatically"
  }'
```

`status` ɗin da aka mayar zai gaya maka inda cycle na farko ya sauka:

- **`active`**: an karɓi invoice na farko. Kana caji yanzu.
- **`trialing`**: price ɗin yana da lokacin gwaji; babu caji tukuna.
- **`past_due`**: caji na farko ya faɗi; [dunning](/guides/dunning-and-recovery)
  shi ne ke kula da dawo da kuɗin yanzu. A kan balance mai sirara, wannan yakan nufi "ba yanzu ba", ba aʼa ba.
- **`incomplete`**: caji ɗin yana buƙatar customer ya ɗauki mataki (matakin OTP na
  card); event ɗin `invoice.action_required` yana ɗauke da sabon mahaɗin checkout.

<Callout type="warning" title="Kada ka ɗauka cewa caji na farko zai yi nasara">
  A Najeriya, pull na farko yana iya buƙatar OTP, ko kuma ya sauka a kan balance
  mai sirara. Ka tanadi `past_due` da `incomplete` tun rana ta farko: ka reshe
  hanyarka bisa webhook, ba bisa `201` kaɗai ba. Wannan shi ne bambanci tsakanin
  abin wasa da tsarin biyan kuɗi na gaskiya.
</Callout>

## Na zaɓi: lokacin gwaji da quantity

- **`trialDays`** a cikin kiran ƙirƙira yana fara lokacin gwaji ko da price ɗin ba shi da ɗaya.
- **`quantity`** yana ninka price na kowane wuri (per-seat).
- **`Idempotency-Key`** yana sa ƙirƙirar ta zama mai aminci ga sake gwadawa: sake
  kira da key ɗaya yana mayar da subscription ɗin nan ɗaya, ba wani na biyu ba.
