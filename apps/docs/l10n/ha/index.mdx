Nomba tana bayar da kayan aikin da suke tura kuɗi. **Nomba One** ita ce sashen da take
mayar da tura kuɗi _sau ɗaya_ zuwa gudanar da kasuwancin subscription (plans, cycles,
proration, dunning, reconciliation, da settlement), domin kada wata tawaga ta sake gina
shi. Yana aiki a kan kowane rail (card, direct debit, transfer, crypto), an kuma gina
shi bisa yadda kuɗi ke motsi a nan da gaske: balance mai ƙaranci, transfer da farko,
inda charge da ya faskara yakan nufi _"ba yanzu ba,"_ ba _"aʼa"_ ba.

Waɗannan takardu **ana iya gudanar da su, kuma gaskiya ne**. Za ka iya kallon
subscription yana biya, ya faskara saboda balance mai ƙaranci, sannan ya farfaɗo ta
hanyar dunning (a kan sandbox na gaske, cikin devtools naka) kafin ka rubuta layi ɗaya.
Kowane error zai gaya maka daidai abin da za ka yi, kuma sassan da suka fi wahala
(dunning, reconciliation, da tarkon biya sau biyu) suna a bayyane cikin takardun.

<Callout type="note" title="Kuɗi kobo ne cikakke">
  Kowane adadi a cikin API lamba ce cikakkiya ta **kobo** (₦1 = kobo 100). `250000` shi
  ne ₦2,500.00. Babu float, babu decimal string. Ana kiran kowane field na kuɗi da
  `…InKobo` domin kada a kuskure maʼaunin.
</Callout>

## An gina shi don a tabbatar, ba don a amince kawai ba

Sauran takardun suna zuwa (cikakkun jagorori, simulator na subscription, error
reference, da **sassan da suka fi wahala** cikin gaskiya), kowanne kuma za a iya
gudanar da shi a kan sandbox domin ka tabbatar yana aiki maimakon ka amince da maganarmu
kawai. Fara da quickstart da ke sama; za ka ga `201` na gaske kafin ka ɗauki wani mataki.
