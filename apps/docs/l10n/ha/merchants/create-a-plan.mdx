**Plan** shi ne abin da customer ɗinka ke yin subscription a kansa, **price** ɗinsa
kuma shi ne yawan kuɗin da kake caji da kuma sau nawa. Kana saita su duka sau ɗaya,
daga console, sannan ka sanya customer a kansu.

## Matakai

<Steps>
  <Step title="Buɗe Plans a cikin console">
    Ka shiga sannan ka je **Plans**. Zaɓi **New plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ba plan ɗin suna">
    Ba shi suna da customer ɗinka za su gane a rasitin ɗinsu: "Pro",
    "Monthly membership", "Premium". Sunan shi ne kayan; kuɗin yana biyo baya.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ƙara price">
    Saita **amount** (misali ₦2,500) da **interval**: sau nawa yake caji. Zaɓi
    maʼauni — minute, day, week, month ko year — da adadinsu, don haka "kowane wata
    uku" yana nufin month interval mai count 3. Kana iya ƙara price fiye da ɗaya a
    plan guda, ta yadda kaya guda za a iya caji shi wata-wata *da kuma*
    shekara-shekara.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ƙara free trial (na zaɓi)">
    Idan kana son sabbin customer su gwada kafin su biya, saita **trial** na adadin
    kwanaki. Ba za a caje su ba sai trial ɗin ya ƙare.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ajiye">
    Ajiye plan ɗin. Yanzu ya shirya don sanya customer a kansa, ta hanyar
    [payment link](/merchants/share-a-payment-link) ko daga jerin customer ɗinka.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Kana iya canza price daga baya, cikin aminci">
  Ƙara price ko cire shi ba ya taɓa dagula customer da suka riga suka yi
  subscription: suna ci gaba da price ɗin da suka shiga a kansa har sai ka matsar da
  su. Don haka babu haɗari a gwada sababbin price.
</Callout>

<Callout type="note" title="Adadin kuɗi koyaushe a naira yake, kuma ana nuna shi daidai">
  Ka shigar da adadin naira da kake son caji (₦2,500). A bayan fage injin yana aiki
  da kobo domin daidaito, amma console yana nunawa kuma yana karɓar naira. Ba lallai
  ne ka taɓa tunanin hakan ba.
</Callout>
