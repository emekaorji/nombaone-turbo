Hanya mafi sauri da za ka sa customer a kan plan ita ce **hanyar haɗin biyan
kuɗi**. Kana ƙirƙirar ta sau ɗaya don plan, ka raba ta ta duk inda kake magana da
customers (WhatsApp, imel, maɓalli a shafinka), kuma duk wanda ya buɗe ta zai yi
rijista ya biya. Daga nan, ana ci gaba da cajin kuɗi ta atomatik a kowane cycle.

## Matakai

<Steps>
  <Step title="Zaɓi plan ɗin">
    A cikin console, buɗe [plan](/merchants/create-a-plan) da kake son karɓar
    kuɗinsa, sannan ka zaɓi **Share link** (ko **Payment link**).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kwafi hanyar haɗin">
    Console yana ba ka hanyar haɗi da aka ɗaura kan wannan plan da price. Za ka
    iya sake amfani da ita: hanyar haɗi ɗaya tana aiki ga kowane customer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Raba ta">
    Aika ta ko ina. Idan customer ya buɗe ta, sai ya shigar da bayanansa, ya zaɓi
    yadda zai biya (card, bank transfer, ko direct debit), sannan ya tabbatar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Biyan kuɗi na ci gaba da kansa">
    Da zarar sun biya a karo na farko, Nomba One zai riƙa cajin su a kowane cycle
    ta atomatik. Ba sai ka sake aika hanyar haɗin ba.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="note" title="Customers na iya biya yadda ʼyan Najeriya suka saba">
  Hanyar haɗin tana goyon bayan rails da ʼyan Najeriya ke amfani da su da gaske:
  **bank transfer** (su aika kuɗi zuwa asusun da aka nuna musu), **card**, da
  **direct debit**. Kai ba ka zaɓa musu. Su kansu suke zaɓar abin da ya dace da
  su.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" title="Card na iya neman lambar sirri ta lokaci ɗaya">
  Wasu bankuna na neman OTP daga customer a karo na farko, ko kuma idan sabuntawa
  na buƙatar sake tabbatarwa. Nomba One yana lura da wannan a madadinka. Idan
  sabuntawa na buƙatar customer ya tabbatar, sai a aika masa sabuwar hanyar haɗi.
  Duba [saƙonnin dunning](/merchants/set-up-dunning-messages).
</Callout>
