Idan ka taɓa haɗa Flutterwave, babban canjin da zai gamsar da kai shi ne:
**reference guda ɗaya maimakon biyu**. Inda Flutterwave ke ba ka `tx_ref` da kai
kake ƙirƙira da kuma `flw_ref` da shi yake ƙirƙira (sai ka riƙa daidaita su da
juna), Nomba One yana ba kowane resource ID guda ɗaya na `nbo…` da ledger
postings ɗinsa da webhooks ɗinsa duk suke amfani da shi.

## Taswirar maʼanoni

| Flutterwave | Nomba One | Lura |
|---|---|---|
| Payment Plan | [Plan](/reference/plans) + [Price](/reference/prices) | Adadin kuɗi da tsakanin lokaci suna zaune kan price. |
| `tx_ref` (naka) + `flw_ref` (nasa) | reference `nbo…` guda ɗaya | ID guda ɗaya ga kowane resource, ko ina. Duba [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger). |
| Subscriptions kan plan | [Subscriptions](/reference/subscriptions) | Tunani iri ɗaya, amma da tsarin rayuwa mai wadata. |
| `amount` (naira, float) | `unitAmountInKobo` (kobo cikakke) | Babu float: `250000` = ₦2,500.00. |
| Webhook `event` | [Katalogin events](/webhooks/event-catalog) | An bayyana nauʼinsu, an rubuta su, an kuma sa musu sa hannu. |

## Abubuwa uku da suka canja

- **Reference guda ɗaya.** Ka daina daidaita `tx_ref` da `flw_ref`. Da kowane ID
  na `nbo…` za ka iya samun resource ɗin, ledger postings ɗinsa, da events
  ɗinsa. Wannan yana kawar da dukan nauʼin kurakuran nan na “wane ID zan ajiye?”.
- **Kobo cikakke, babu float.** Adadin kuɗin Flutterwave yana zuwa da naira,
  kuma sau da yawa float ne. Nomba One kobo cikakke ne kullum. Duba
  [kuɗi kobo cikakke ne](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo) ka kuma yi hankali da
  [tarkon 100×](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo) saʼad da kake mayar da adadin kuɗi.
- **Tabbatar da webhooks ta HMAC kan raw body.** Ka maye gurbin duban header na
  `verif-hash` da [tsarin sa hannu](/webhooks/signing-and-verification):
  HMAC-SHA256 kan `timestamp.rawBody`, sannan ka kwatanta da hanyar da lokacinta
  ba ya sauyawa.

<Callout type="tip" title="Bank transfer hanya ce ta gaske, ba mafita ta gefe ba">
  Flutterwave yana ɗaukar transfer a matsayin charge na lokaci ɗaya da za ka
  ɗinke cikin plan. A Nomba One kuwa transfer rail ne na gaskiya na subscription.
  Injin yana nuna inda za a biya, yana kuma yin settlement da zarar kuɗin ya iso.
  Duba [multi-rail: turawa da jawowa](/concepts/multi-rail-push-and-pull).
</Callout>
