Ba kwa buƙatar yankewa kwatsam kafin ku koma Nomba One. Hanya mafi aminci ita ce a
gudanar da processor guda biyu a lokaci ɗaya: sabbin subscription su fara a Nomba One,
yayin da waɗanda suke akwai za su ci gaba da biya a tsohon tsarin har sai sun kai
lokacin sabuntawa sun tsallaka. Babu abin da zai lalace tsakiyar aikin, kuma kuna iya
tsayawa a kowane lokaci.

## Tsarin aikin

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sake gina katalogin ku a Nomba One">
    Sake ƙirƙirar [plans da prices](/guides/create-plans-and-prices) ɗinku da test key.
    Adadin kuɗi yana cikin [integer kobo](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo). Ku tabbatar
    lambobin naira sun yi daidai kafin ku taɓa live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Haɗa webhooks kuma ku tabbatar da su">
    Yi rijistar endpoint ɗinku sannan ku tabbatar da tantance sa hannu da
    [live secret](/webhooks/signing-and-verification). Yi amfani da
    [tabbatar da mu a cikin devtools](/getting-started/verify-in-your-devtools) don
    tabbatar da bututun kafin event na gaskiya su fara gudana.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tura sabbin subscription zuwa Nomba One">
    Ku kai sabbin masu rajista zuwa Nomba One, yayin da subscription da suke akwai suke
    ci gaba da biya a tsohon processor. Nan ne farkon aikin, ba tare da wani haɗari ga
    customer na yanzu ba.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Matsar da customer da suke akwai yayin da suke sabuntawa">
    Duk lokacin da tsohuwar subscription ta kai lokacin sabuntawa, sai ku fara cycle
    ɗinta na gaba a Nomba One maimakon tsohon wurin. Matsawa a kan iyakar sabuntawa yana
    hana karɓar kuɗi sau biyu da kuma matsalar proration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Kwashe tsohon processor ku kashe shi">
    Idan tsohon tsarin bai sauran wata subscription mai aiki, sai ku kashe shi. Ba a taɓa
    yin yankewa kwatsam ba — mikawa kawai aka yi.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dokokin da ke kiyaye lafiyar aikin

<Callout type="warning" title="Kada ku taɓa cajin customer a wurare biyu lokaci guda">
  Customer dole ne ya kasance mai aiki a processor guda ɗaya kacal a kowane lokaci. Ku
  matsar a kan iyakar sabuntawa, sannan ku soke tsohuwar subscription a daidai lokacin da
  sabuwar ta fara — ba kafin ba, ba bayan ba.
</Callout>

- **Idempotency-Key a kan kowace kira da ke motsa kuɗi**, domin sake gwadawa yayin
  matsawar kada ya haifar da caji sau biyu. Duba [zuwa live](/guides/going-live).
- **Ku yi reconciliation daga ledger, ba daga tunani ba.** Bayan matsar da customer,
  [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger) shi ne tushen gaskiya kan abin da suka biya.
- **Ku bar tsohon webhook handler yana aiki** har sai tsohon processor ya kare gaba ɗaya:
  event da suka makara suna buƙatar sarrafawa.

<Callout type="tip" title="Card token ba safai suke tsallakawa ba">
  Card token da aka ajiye ba safai suke iya tsallakawa daga wannan processor zuwa wancan
  ba. Ku shirya sake karɓar bayanan biyan kuɗi a kan iyakar sabuntawa
  ([payment link](/guides/start-a-subscription) ko matakin saita card) maimakon zaton
  token za su tsallaka.
</Callout>
