Idan ka riga kana karɓar kuɗin subscription a kan Paystack, Stripe, ko
Flutterwave, ba za ka fara daga sifili ba: yawancin kaʼidoji suna daidaita kai
tsaye, kuma waɗanda ba sa daidaita, galibi Nomba One ne ya cire wani tarko da ka
koyi rayuwa da shi. Wannan sashe yana daidaita yadda kake tunani a yanzu da namu,
kuma yana ba ka hanya mai aminci ta sauyawa ba tare da tsayawar aiki ba.

## Abin da yake iri ɗaya a koʼina

Duk inda kake fitowa, waɗannan suna daidaita a fili:

- **Plan da price**: samfurin da adadin kuɗin da ake maimaitawa.
- **Customer**: wanda kake karɓar kuɗi a wurinsa.
- **Subscription**: customer a kan wani price, ana caji a kowane cycle.
- **Webhook**: event ɗin da ke gaya wa server ɗinka abin da ya faru.

## Abin da Nomba One ke yi daban (da gangan)

- **Ishara guda ɗaya, a koʼina.** Wani resource, ledger postings ɗinsa, da webhook
  ɗinsa duk suna raba id `nbo…` guda ɗaya, babu rikicewar `tx_ref` da `flw_ref` da
  `id`. Duba [ledger](/concepts/the-ledger).
- **Kobo a matsayin cikakken lamba, kullum.** Babu float, babu decimal string mai
  shakku: kowane field na kuɗi yana ƙarewa da `InKobo`. Duba
  [kuɗi cikakken kobo ne](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo).
- **Gaskiya game da sassa masu wahala.** Balance mara isashe, OTP na card, da rails
  na push-vs-pull duk suna cikin manyan abubuwan da muka tsara, ba wasu ƙananan
  matsalolin da za ka gano bayan ka shiga production ba. Duba
  [kaʼidojin](/concepts/how-billing-works).
