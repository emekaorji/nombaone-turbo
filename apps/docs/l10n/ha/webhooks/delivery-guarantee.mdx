nombaone yana tabbatar da delivery **aƙalla sau ɗaya** (at-least-once), kuma yana
faɗin haka a kan kowace delivery cikin header `X-Nombaone-Delivery-Guarantee`. Zaɓi
ne da muka yi da gangan kuma cikin gaskiya: delivery na daidai sau ɗaya kacal a kan
hanyar sadarwa marar tabbas ba abu ne mai yiwuwa ba, kuma duk API da ke iƙirarin
hakan ƙarya yake yi. Abin da muke ba ka a maimakon haka shi ne tabbaci da za ka iya
gina aiki a kansa, tare da kayan aiki guda ɗaya da ke sa ya zama mai aminci: event
id tsayayye da za ka tace kwafi da shi.

## Maʼanar aƙalla sau ɗaya a gare ka

Event guda **na iya** iso fiye da sau ɗaya: retry bayan amsa mai jinkiri, matsalar
hanyar sadarwa, ko sake aikawa. Dole handler ɗinka ya ɗauki maimaici a matsayin abin
da ba ya yin komai.

<Callout type="warning" title="Ka tace kwafi bisa event id, bayan tantancewa">
  Ka adana kowane `event.id` da ka riga ka sarrafa, ka kuma tsallake maimaici. Ka yi
  wannan **bayan** tantance signature, don kada body da ba a tantance ba ya taɓa
  gurɓata ajiyar dedupe ɗinka.
</Callout>

```ts
if (await seen(event.id)) return respond(200); // already handled, no-op
await markSeen(event.id);
await handle(event);
```

## Dalilin da ya sa idempotency dole ta kai har ƙasa

Tace kwafi a gefe kaɗai bai isa ba: aikin da handler ɗin ke jawowa shi kansa dole ya
zama idempotent, saboda
[retry na webhook ba retry na charge ba ne](/concepts/hard-parts/retry-the-webhook-is-not-retry-the-charge).
Idan kwafin `invoice.paid` ya kuɓuce, ƙara kuɗi cikin balance sau biyu babbar matsala
ce ta gaske. [Ledger](/concepts/the-ledger) yana tilasta wannan tun daga ƙasa: ana
neman motsin kuɗi sau ɗaya kacal, don haka ko da an sarrafa event sau biyu, posting
guda ɗaya ne kawai zai fito.

## Retry da tsari

- **Retry.** Delivery da ba ta samu `2xx` a kan lokaci ba, ana sake gwada ta bisa
  jadawalin backoff. Ka mayar da `2xx` da sauri, ka yi aikin a async don kada handler
  mai jinkiri ya jawo retry da ba lallai ba ne.
- **Ba a tabbatar da tsari ba.** Event na iya iso ba bisa jeri ba. Kada ka ɗauka cewa
  `invoice.paid` zai iso kafin `subscription.activated`. Ka duba resource ɗin kai
  tsaye (`GET`) idan tsari yana da muhimmanci, maimakon ka yanke yanayin daga jerin
  event.
- **Replay.** Za ka iya sake neman delivery da ta wuce da
  `POST /v1/webhooks/{id}/deliveries/{deliveryId}/replay`: event id ɗin guda ne, don
  haka dedupe ɗinka zai ɗauke ta a matsayin wadda aka riga aka gani, kamar yadda ya
  kamata.
