Webhook shi ne yadda nombaone ke gaya wa server ɗinka abin da ya faru ba tare da
ka riƙa tambaya kullum ba. Lokacin da aka biya invoice, ko charge ya faɗi, ko
card ya buƙaci OTP, muna `POST` ɗin event mai sa hannu zuwa endpoint ɗin da ka yi
rijista da shi. Duk abin da ya shafi ɓangaren fita yana cikin wannan sashe;
tsarin handler kuwa yana cikin [sarrafa webhooks](/guides/handle-webhooks).

## Yadda delivery yake

Kowace delivery HTTP `POST` ce mai body na JSON da header na sa hannu:

```http
POST /your/endpoint HTTP/1.1
Content-Type: application/json
X-Nombaone-Signature: t=1719920092,v1=8f3c…
X-Nombaone-Delivery-Guarantee: at-least-once

{
  "id": "evt_2a7e1b0d8c3a",
  "type": "invoice.paid",
  "reference": "nbo749201835566inv",
  "createdAt": "2026-07-02T10:14:52.004Z",
  "data": { "reference": "nbo749201835566inv" }
}
```

`type` shi ne sunan event ɗin, `reference` shi ne id na bainar jamaʼa na resource
ɗin, `id` kuwa shi ne id na musamman na event da za ka yi dedupe da shi.

## Yin rijistar endpoint

Ka nuna webhook zuwa URL da kake sarrafawa, sannan ka zaɓi waɗanne events zai
riƙa karɓa:

```bash
curl -X POST https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/webhooks \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "url": "https://yourapp.com/webhooks/nomba",
    "enabledEvents": ["invoice.paid", "invoice.payment_failed", "invoice.action_required"]
  }'
```

Za ka karɓi **sirrin sa hannu** (`whsec_…`), wanda ake nunawa sau ɗaya kawai.
Endpoints suna aiki bisa mode: event na sandbox zai isa endpoint ɗin sandbox
ɗinka kaɗai.

## Dokoki huɗu

Handler mai kyau kullum yana yin waɗannan abubuwa huɗu, bi da bi:

1. **[Tabbatar da sa hannun](/webhooks/signing-and-verification)** a kan raw body,
   kafin ka yi parsing.
2. **[Yi dedupe bisa id ɗin event](/webhooks/delivery-guarantee)**: delivery yana
   at-least-once.
3. **Ka amsa `2xx` da sauri**, sannan ka yi aikin a hanyar asynchronous.
4. **Ka shirya wa events marasa daɗi**, ba `invoice.paid` kaɗai ba.

<Callout type="tip" title="Ka gan shi kafin ka gina shi">
  Sandbox ba ya aika webhooks da kansa, don haka
  [tabbatar da mu a cikin devtools ɗinka](/getting-started/verify-in-your-devtools)
  yana ba ka event na gaske mai sa hannu, don ka harba shi zuwa endpoint ɗinka ka
  kuma ga tabbatarwar ta yi nasara.
</Callout>
