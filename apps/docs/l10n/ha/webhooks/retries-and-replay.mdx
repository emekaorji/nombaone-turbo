Hanyoyin sadarwa suna kasawa, sabar suna sake farawa, deploy yana faruwa a
tsakiyar buƙata. nombaone yana ɗaukar cewa endpoint ɗinka zai rasa delivery
lokaci-lokaci, kuma an gina shi don farfaɗowa: ta atomatik da sake gwadawa, kuma
da hannu ta hanyar replay. Duka biyun suna amfani da **event id ɗaya**, don haka
handler da yake yin [dedupe](/webhooks/delivery-guarantee) yadda ya kamata zai
ɗauki delivery da aka dawo da shi a matsayin wanda ya riga ya gani.

## Sake gwadawa ta atomatik

Delivery yana yin nasara idan endpoint ɗinka ya mayar da `2xx` ba tare da ɓata
lokaci ba. Duk wani abu banda haka (ƙarewar lokaci, `5xx`, ko kuskuren haɗi) ana
sake gwada shi bisa backoff mai ƙaruwa, wanda ke ba endpoint da ya ɗan faɗi
damar dawowa ba tare da ka yi komai ba.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fara mayar da 2xx, ka yi aikin daga baya">
  Hanya mafi sauri ta guje wa sake gwadawa ita ce ka amince da saƙon nan take
  sannan ka sarrafa shi ta hanyar asynchronous. Handler da yake yin aiki mai
  jinkiri a cikin buƙatar yana kama da kasawa, kuma yana jawo sake gwadawa da
  bai buƙata ba. Ka tabbatar, ka yi dedupe, ka jera shi a layi, sannan ka mayar
  da `200`.
</Callout>

Delivery da ya ci gaba da kasawa har jadawalin sake gwadawa ya ƙare ana yi masa
alama a matsayin wanda ya kasa, kuma ba a ƙara sake gwada shi. Ba ya sake
gwadawa har abada. Kullum kana iya ganin inda delivery yake, ka kuma sake tura
shi da kanka.

## Duba delivery

Ana rubuta kowane gwaji. Ka jera duk delivery na endpoint, ko ka karanta ɗaya:

```bash
# All deliveries for an endpoint
curl https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/webhooks/{id}/deliveries \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"

# One delivery, with its attempts and your endpoint's responses
curl https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/webhooks/{id}/deliveries/{deliveryId} \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"
```

## Yin replay na delivery

Ka sake aika event da ya wuce duk lokacin da kake buƙata, bayan ka gyara
kuskuren handler, ko don cike gurbin endpoint da ya faɗi:

```bash
curl -X POST \
  https://sandbox.api.nombaone.xyz/v1/webhooks/{id}/deliveries/{deliveryId}/replay \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer nbo_sandbox_…"
```

<Callout type="warning" title="Replay yana riƙe event id na asali">
  Replay shi ne event ɗin nan ɗaya, an sake aika shi, ba sabo ba ne. Maʼajiyar
  dedupe ɗinka ya kamata ta gane shi a matsayin wanda aka riga aka sarrafa.
  Wannan shi ne ainihin abin da kake so: replay yana da aminci saboda handler
  ɗinka idempotent ne.
</Callout>

## Ka rasa wani lokaci? Ka yi reconciliation, kada ka sake ginawa

Idan endpoint ɗinka ya faɗi kuma ba ka da tabbas kan abin da ka rasa, kada ka yi
ƙoƙarin sake gina yanayin daga jerin event da kake hasashe. Ka karanta resource
kai tsaye (`GET
/v1/subscriptions/{id}`) ko ka yi replay na takamaiman delivery ɗin. API ce
tushen gaskiya, event kuwa saƙonnin sanarwa ne game da ita.
