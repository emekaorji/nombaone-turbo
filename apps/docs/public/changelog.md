# Changelog

Every change to the Nomba One API, dated and grouped, newest first. Breaking
changes always carry a migration note. This log is generated by diffing the API's
OpenAPI snapshot between releases, so it can't silently omit a change.

> **How versioning works**
>
> The API is versioned by URL prefix (`/v1`). Additive changes — new endpoints,
> new optional fields, new events — ship within `v1` and appear here under
> **Added**. Anything that could break an integration waits for a new major version
> and is called out explicitly.

## 2026-07-01 — v1

The initial public release. The full subscription-billing surface over Nigerian
rails.

### Added

- **Plans & prices** — [`/v1/plans`](/reference/plans) and nested
[`/v1/plans/{id}/prices`](/reference/prices): products, recurring amounts
(integer kobo), intervals, and trials.
- **Customers** — [`/v1/customers`](/reference/customers) with
[credit grants](/reference/credits) and [discounts](/reference/coupons).
- **Subscriptions** — [`/v1/subscriptions`](/reference/subscriptions): the full
lifecycle — create, change (with proration), pause, resume, cancel, resubscribe,
scheduled changes, upcoming-invoice preview, and dunning inspection.
- **Payment methods** — [`/v1/payment-methods`](/reference/payment-methods):
card setup, [mandates](/reference/mandates) (direct debit), and virtual accounts
(bank transfer) — one subscription over any rail.
- **Invoices** — [`/v1/invoices`](/reference/invoices): per-cycle invoices with
line items, discounts, and credits, backed by a
[double-entry ledger](/concepts/the-ledger).
- **Coupons** — [`/v1/coupons`](/reference/coupons): percent or fixed discounts
with a duration.
- **Settlements** — [`/v1/settlements`](/reference/settlements): refunds (tenant
leg only), escrow reads, and payouts to a bank account.
- **Webhooks** — [`/v1/webhooks`](/reference/webhooks) with signed delivery, a full
[event catalog](/webhooks/event-catalog), deliveries, and replay.
- **Events** — [`/v1/events`](/reference/events): the domain-event stream and its
machine-readable catalog.
- **Organization & metrics** — [`/v1/organization`](/reference/organization) and
[`/v1/metrics`](/reference/metrics): config, billing settings, MRR, churn, and the
dunning funnel.
- **Test toolkit** — [`/v1/test/*`](/test-toolkit/overview): deterministic payment
methods, a test clock, and on-demand signed webhook simulation (sandbox only).
- **Agent-native docs** — a [Markdown mirror](/agents) of every page, `llms.txt`,
and `llms-full.txt`.

### Notes

- **Money is integer kobo** throughout — every money field ends in `InKobo`. See
[money is integer kobo](/concepts/money-is-integer-kobo).
- **Idempotency** — every money-moving `POST` accepts an `Idempotency-Key`.
- **Delivery is at-least-once** — dedupe webhooks on the event id. See the
[delivery guarantee](/webhooks/delivery-guarantee).
