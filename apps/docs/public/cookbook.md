# Cookbook

## The first recipe is here

- **[Bill a gym membership](/cookbook/bill-a-gym-membership)**: 
The flagship end-to-end recipe: connect your Nomba account, one plan with
three prices, members joining through hosted checkout, silent card renewals
with an honest OTP fallback, transfer memberships on the invoice lane, and
dunning that recovers before it churns.

More are on the way. Until they land, two places already cover the ground a recipe would:

- The [guides](/guides/create-plans-and-prices) walk through the core flows, from creating a plan to going live, end to end.
- The [API reference](/reference/customers) documents every endpoint a recipe would call, with a runnable request builder on each resource.

Have a recipe you want next? Tell us on the [changelog](/changelog) thread, and we will prioritise it.
