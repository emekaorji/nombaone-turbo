# Share a payment link

The fastest way to put a customer on a plan is a **payment link**. You create it
once for a plan, share it however you talk to customers (WhatsApp, email, a
button on your site), and each person who opens it subscribes and pays. Billing
then continues automatically every cycle.

## Steps

### Pick the plan

In the console, open the [plan](/merchants/create-a-plan) you want to collect
and choose **Share link** (or **Payment link**).

### Copy the link

The console gives you a link tied to that plan and price. It's reusable: the
same link works for every customer.

### Share it

Send it anywhere. When a customer opens it, they enter their details, choose
how to pay (card, bank transfer, or direct debit), and confirm.

### Billing starts on its own

Once they've paid the first time, Nomba One bills them every cycle
automatically. You don't send the link again.

> **Customers can pay the Nigerian way**
>
> The link supports the rails Nigerians actually use: **bank transfer** (they send
> money to a shown account), **card**, and **direct debit**. You don't choose for
> them. They pick what suits them.

> **A card may ask for a one-time code**
>
> Some banks ask the customer for an OTP the first time, or when a renewal needs
> re-confirming. Nomba One handles this for you. If a renewal needs the customer
> to confirm, it messages them a fresh link. See
> [dunning messages](/merchants/set-up-dunning-messages).

- **[Set up dunning messages](/merchants/set-up-dunning-messages)**: 
Decide what customers see if a payment fails.
- **[Read a settlement](/merchants/read-a-settlement)**: 
Find the money after they've paid.
