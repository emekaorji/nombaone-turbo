Geist for everything, Vercel's own grotesk. The marketing scale runs big and confident: display through H3 carry tight negative tracking, and the mono carries code, data, and captions. Patterns

    
The layout laws the marketing site holds to. Tokens and components above are the vocabulary; these are the grammar. Big type and generous space are the signal, the calm confidence of a team that is sure of what it built.


    
Layout

    

      

        ◆
        Whitespace, never dividers. Sections are separated by generous whitespace, with section vertical padding of 128px and up, never by hairline rules. A divider reads as a document; open space reads as a product.
      

      

        ◆
        No eyebrows. A section header is a title plus one short deck, and nothing else. The mono-uppercase eyebrow is retired across the site.
      

    


    
Hero glow: a background fill, not a floating ellipse

    

      

        radial-gradient · dark-emerald center to near-black edge
      

      

        ◆
        Paint it as a background. The emerald glow is a background radial-gradient fill on the hero frame, dark-emerald at the center fading to near-black at the edge. It is never an absolute or floating ellipse. Overlay elements paint above content in both Pencil and CSS stacking, so a glow laid over the headline would wash it out. This is a build note, not a preference.
